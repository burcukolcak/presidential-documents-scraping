George Washington
December 20, 1791

Special Message
Gentlemen of the Senate and of the House of Representatives:
I lay before you the copy of a letter which I have received from the governor of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, and of sundry documents which accompanied it, relative to contract for the purchase of a certain tract of land bounding on Lake Erie, together with a copy of a report of the Secretary of State on the same subject.
GO. WASHINGTON.
