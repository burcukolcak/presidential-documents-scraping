John Tyler
April 13, 1842

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 24th of July last, I communicate to that body a report from the Secretary of State, conveying copies of the correspondence* which contains the information called for by that resolution.
JOHN TYLER.
* Of the diplomatic agent and minister of the United States at the Court of Austria relative to the commercial interests of the United States.
