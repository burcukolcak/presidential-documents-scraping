John Tyler
December 07, 1841

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith a report from the War Department, in compliance with so much of the resolution of the Senate of March 3, 1841, respecting the military and naval defenses of the country, as relates to the defenses under the superintendence of that Department.
JOHN TYLER.
