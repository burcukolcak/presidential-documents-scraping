John Tyler
December 29, 1842

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the Senate a report** from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers, in answer to their resolution of the 27th instant.
JOHN TYLER.
**Transmitting correspondence between the United States minister at London and the British Government in relation to certain slaves taken from the wreck of the schoonerHermosa and liberated by the authorities at Nassau, New Providence.
