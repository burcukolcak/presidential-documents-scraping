John Tyler
February 08, 1843

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the House of Representatives, in answer to their resolution of the 28th ultimo, a report* from the Secretary of State.
JOHN TYLER.
*Stating that no information is in possession of the Government of any negotiation of a treaty, or of any overtures to treat, for a cession of California by Mexico to England.
