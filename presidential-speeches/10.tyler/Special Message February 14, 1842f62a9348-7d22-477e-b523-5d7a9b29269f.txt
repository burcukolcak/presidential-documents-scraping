John Tyler
February 14, 1842

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 3d instant, I transmit herewith a report ** from the Secretary of State, with copies of the papers requested by the resolution.
JOHN TYLER.
**Relating to letters written in March, 1841 by Andrew Stevenson, United States minister at the Court of Great Britain, to Isaac Hull, commander of the United States squadron in the Mediterranean, which caused a part of that squadron to return to the United States.
