John Tyler
February 21, 1844

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the House of Representatives, in answer to their resolution of the 16th instant, a report * from the Secretary of State, with the correspondence therein referred to.
JOHN TYLER.
* Relating to a demand upon the British Government for the surrender of certain fugitive criminals from Florida under the provisions of the tenth article of the treaty of Washington.
