John Tyler
February 21, 1845

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the Senate, in answer to their resolution of the 14th instant, a report of the Secretary of State, with the accompanying papers.*
JOHN TYLER.
*Instructions to Hon. Caleb Cushing. commissioner to China and envoy extraordinary and minister plenipotentiary to the Court of China, etc.
