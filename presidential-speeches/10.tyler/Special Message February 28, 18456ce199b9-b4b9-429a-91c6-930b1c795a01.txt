John Tyler
February 28, 1845

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith to the Senate, in answer to its resolution of the 17th instant, a report* from the Secretary of State, together with the copies of papers therein referred to.
JOHN TYLER.
*Relating to redress from the British Government for the illegal capture of the fishing schoonerArgus and other American vessels engaged in the fisheries, under a pretended infraction of the convention of October 20, 1818.
