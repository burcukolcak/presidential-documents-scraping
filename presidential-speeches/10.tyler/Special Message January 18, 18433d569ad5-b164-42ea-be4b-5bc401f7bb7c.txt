John Tyler
January 18, 1843

Special Message
To the House of Representatives.
In compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 27th ultimo, I now transmit the letter and pamphlet* which accompanies this.
JOHN TYLER.
*Entitled "Acts and Resolutions of the Legislative Council of the Territory of Florida," passed at its twentieth session, January 3 to March 5, 1842.
