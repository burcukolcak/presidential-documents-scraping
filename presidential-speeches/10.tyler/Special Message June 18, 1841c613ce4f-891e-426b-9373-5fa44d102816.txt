John Tyler
June 18, 1841

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I have the honor to transmit to the Senate the accompanying letter* from the Secretary of the Treasury, in pursuance of its resolution of the 8th instant.
JOHN TYLER.
*Relating to allowances since March 4, 1841, of claims arising under the invasion of East Florida
APP Note:  The date of this document is approximate.  It was received and published in theJournal of the Senateon Monday June 21. We record Friday June 18.
