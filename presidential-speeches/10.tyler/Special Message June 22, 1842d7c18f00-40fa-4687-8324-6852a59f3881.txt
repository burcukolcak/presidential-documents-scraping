John Tyler
June 22, 1842

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 15th of April last, I communicate to the Senate a report from the Secretary of State, accompanying copies of the correspondence* called for by said resolution.
JOHN TYLER.
* Relating to the conduct and character of William B. Hodgson (nominated to be consul at Tunis) while dragoman at Constantinople.
