James K. Polk
December 16, 1845

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I herewith transmit a report from the Secretary of State, containing the information called for by the resolution of the Senate of the 8th of January last, in relation to the claim of the owners of the brigGeneralArmstrongagainst the Government of Portugal.*
JAMES K. POLK.
*For failing to protect the American armed brigGeneral Armstrong , while lying in the port of Fayal, Azores, from attack by British armed ships on September 26, 1814.
