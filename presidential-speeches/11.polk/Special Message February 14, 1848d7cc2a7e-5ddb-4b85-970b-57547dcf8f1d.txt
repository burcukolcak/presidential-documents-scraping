James K. Polk
February 14, 1848

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit, for the consideration of the Senate with a view to ratification, a treaty of peace, friendship, commerce, and navigation between the United States and the Republic of Peru, concluded and signed in this city on the 9th instant by the Secretary of State and the minister plenipotentiary of Peru, in behalf of their respective Governments. also transmit a copy of the correspondence between them which led to the treaty.
JAMES K. POLK.
