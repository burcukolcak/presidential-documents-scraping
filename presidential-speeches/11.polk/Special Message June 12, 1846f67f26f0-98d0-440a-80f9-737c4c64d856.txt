James K. Polk
June 12, 1846

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith for the information of Congress, official reports received at the War Department from the officer commanding the Army on the Mexican frontier, giving a detailed report of the operations of the Army in that quarter, and particularly of the recent engagements* between the American and Mexican forces.
JAMES K. POLK.
*Palo Alto and Resaca de la Palma.
