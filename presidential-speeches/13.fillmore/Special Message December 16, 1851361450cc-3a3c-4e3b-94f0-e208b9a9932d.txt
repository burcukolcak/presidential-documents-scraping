Millard Fillmore
December 16, 1851

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 8th instant, requesting the communication of a dispatch * addressed to the Department of State by Mr. Niles, late charge' d'affaires of the United States at Turin, I transmit a report from the Secretary of State, which is accompanied by a copy of the dispatch.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
*On the subject of a ship canal between the Atlantic and Pacific oceans.
