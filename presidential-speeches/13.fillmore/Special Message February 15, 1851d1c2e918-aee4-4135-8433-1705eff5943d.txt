Millard Fillmore
February 15, 1851

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the Senate a report++from the Secretary of State, in answer to their resolution of the 10th instant.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
++Relating to taxation by New Granada on United States citizens when in transit across the Isthmus of Panama, and to the United States mail service at said Isthmus.
