Millard Fillmore
June 22, 1852

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, with the accompanying documents,* in compliance with the Senate's resolution of the 29th of April last.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
*Correspondence of the American charge' at Vienna on the subject of the apprehension and Imprisonment by the Austrian authorities of Rev. Charles L. Brace, an American citizen.
