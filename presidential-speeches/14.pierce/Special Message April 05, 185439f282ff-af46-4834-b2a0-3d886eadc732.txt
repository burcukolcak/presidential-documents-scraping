Franklin Pierce
April 05, 1854

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I transmit herewith to the House of Representatives a report of the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents,* in further compliance with their resolution of the 10th of March, 1854.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
*Relating to violations of the rights of American citizens by Spanish authorities and their refusal to allow United States vessels to enter ports of Cuba, etc.
