Franklin Pierce
April 30, 1856

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith to the House of Representatives a report* from the Secretary of State, in answer to their resolution of the 7th instant.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
*Relating to indemnification by the Spanish Government if the captains, owners, and crews the barkGeorgiana and the brigSusan Loudfor their capture and confiscation by the Spanish, authorities.
