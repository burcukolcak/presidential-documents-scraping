Franklin Pierce
December 12, 1853

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In answer to the resolutions of the Senate of the 17th of August, 1852, and 23d of February last, requesting a copy of correspondence relative to the claim on the Government of Portugal in the case of the brigGeneral Armstrong, I transmit a report from the Secretary of State, to whose Department the resolutions were referred.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
