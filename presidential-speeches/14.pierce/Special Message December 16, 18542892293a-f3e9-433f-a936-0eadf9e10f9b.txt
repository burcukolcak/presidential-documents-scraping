Franklin Pierce
December 16, 1854

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers, ++ in answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 27th of July last.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
++ Relating to the case of Walter M. Gibson, held in duress by the Dutch authorities at Batavia, island of Java, on a charge of having attempted to excite the native chiefs of Sumatra to throw off their allegiance to the Dutch Government.
