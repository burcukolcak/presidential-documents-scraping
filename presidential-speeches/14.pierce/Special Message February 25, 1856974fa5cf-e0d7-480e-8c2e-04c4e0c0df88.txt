Franklin Pierce
February 25, 1856

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I communicate to the Senate herewith, for its constitutional action thereon, a treaty made and concluded on the 17th October, 1855, by and between A. Gumming and Isaac I. Stevens, commissioners on the part of the United States, and the Blackfeet and other tribes of Indians on the Upper Missouri and Yellowstone rivers.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
