Franklin Pierce
February 26, 1855

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith a report of the Secretary of the Navy, in compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 20th instant, requesting the President "to communicate to the Senate a copy of the order issued by the Navy Department to the officer in command of the Home Squadron in pursuance of which the United States sloop of warAlbanywas ordered on her last cruise to Carthagerio and Aspinwall, etc.; also of the orders given by such officer to Commander Gerry to proceed upon such cruise, and also of any reports or letters from the captain of theAlbanyon the necessity of repairs to said vessel."
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
