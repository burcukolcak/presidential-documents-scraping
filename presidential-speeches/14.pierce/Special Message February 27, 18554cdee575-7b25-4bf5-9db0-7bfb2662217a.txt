Franklin Pierce
February 27, 1855

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States:
I communicate herewith, for the consideration of Congress, a letter of this date from the Secretary of the Interior, and accompanying paper, recommending certain appropriations + on account of the Indian service.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
+ For running the boundary line between the Chickasaw and Choctaw nations of Indians and for negotiations with the Menominee Indians.
