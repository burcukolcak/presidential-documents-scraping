Franklin Pierce
March 03, 1855

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith to the House of Representatives a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents,++ in answer to their resolutions of the 30th of January and 23d February last.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
++Correspondence relative to the causes disturbing the friendly relations between Spain and the United States diplomatic agents relative to the same; correspondence relative to Cuba, etc.
