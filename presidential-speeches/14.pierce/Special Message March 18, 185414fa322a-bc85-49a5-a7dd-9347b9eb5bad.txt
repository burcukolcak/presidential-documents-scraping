Franklin Pierce
March 18, 1854

Special Message
Speaker of the House of Representatives:
SIR: I transmit to you herewith a report of the present date from the Secretary of the Interior, accompanied by a tabular statement containing the information* called for by resolution of the House of Representatives adopted the 13th ultimo.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
*Area of each State and Territory; extent of the public domain remaining in each state and Territory, and the extent alienated by sales, grants, etc.
