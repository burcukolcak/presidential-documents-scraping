James Buchanan
May 03, 1858

Message to the House of Representatives on Indian Affairs in Oregon and Washington
To the House of Representatives:
In compliance with the resolutions of the House of Representatives of the 19th January, 1857, and 3d February, 1858, I herewith transmit the report of the Secretary of the Interior, with accompanying documents.*
JAMES BUCHANAN.
*Relating to Indian affairs in Oregon and Washington Territories and to the official conduct of Anson Dart, superintendent of Indian affairs in Oregon Territory.
