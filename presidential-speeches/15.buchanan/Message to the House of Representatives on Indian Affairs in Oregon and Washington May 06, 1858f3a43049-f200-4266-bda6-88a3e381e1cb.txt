James Buchanan
May 06, 1858

Message to the House of Representatives on Indian Affairs in Oregon and Washington
To the House of Representatives:
In compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 3d of February, 1858, I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of War, with all papers and correspondence* so far as the same is afforded by the files of the Department.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
*Relating to Indian affairs in Oregon and Washington Territories and to the official conduct of Anson Dart, superintendent of Indian affairs in Oregon Territory.
