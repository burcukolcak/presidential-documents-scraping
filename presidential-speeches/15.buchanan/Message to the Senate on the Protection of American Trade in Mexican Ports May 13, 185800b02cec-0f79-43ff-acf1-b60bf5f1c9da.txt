James Buchanan
May 13, 1858

Message to the Senate on the Protection of American Trade in Mexican Ports
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit to the Senate a report, dated 13th instant, with the accompanying papers, received from the Secretary of State in answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 5th instant, requesting information in regard to measures which may have been adopted for the protection of American commerce in the ports of Mexico.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
