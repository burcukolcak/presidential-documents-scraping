James Buchanan
May 29, 1858

Message to the Senate on the Seizure of the Vessel Panchita
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers, in answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 22d instant, requesting information in regard to the seizure of the American vesselPanchitaon the coast of Africa.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
