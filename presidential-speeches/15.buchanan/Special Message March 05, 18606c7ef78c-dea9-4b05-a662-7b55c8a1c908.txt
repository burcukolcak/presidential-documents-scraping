James Buchanan
March 05, 1860

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the Senate of the 23d of February, 1860, I transmit to that body a communication* of the Secretary of War, furnishing all the information requested in said resolution.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
*Relating to disturbances on the Rio Grande between citizens and military authorities of Mexico and Texas.
