Andrew Johnson
February 04, 1869

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 23d January ultimo, I transmit a report* of the Secretary of State, which is accompanied by a copy of the correspondence called for by the resolution.
ANDREW JOHNSON.
*Relating to the claim of William T. Harris, a United States citizen. to property withheld by the Brazilian Government.
