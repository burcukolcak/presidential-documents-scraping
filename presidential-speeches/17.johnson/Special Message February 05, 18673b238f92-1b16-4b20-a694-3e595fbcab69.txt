Andrew Johnson
February 05, 1867

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith, in answer to the Senate's resolution of the 2d instant, a report from the Secretary of State, with an accompanying document.*
ANDREW JOHNSON.
*Copy of the letter on which the Secretary of State rounded his inquiries addressed to Mr. Motley. United States minister at Vienna, with regard to his reported conversation and opinions.
