Andrew Johnson
January 29, 1867

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I transmit herewith a report* from the Secretary of State, in answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 24th instant.
ANDREW JOHNSON.
*Stating that the Department of State has received no information concerning the removal of the Protestant Church or religious assembly meeting at the American embassy from the city of Rome by an order of that Government.
