Andrew Johnson
March 11, 1867

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit to the Senate, in answer to their resolution of the 28th of July last, a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents.*
ANDREW JOHNSON.
*Correspondence since March 4. 1857, touching the claim to military service asserted by France and Prussia in reference to persons born in those countries, but who have since become citizens of the United States.
