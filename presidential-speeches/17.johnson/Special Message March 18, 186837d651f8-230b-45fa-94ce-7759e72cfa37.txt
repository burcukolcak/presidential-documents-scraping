Andrew Johnson
March 18, 1868

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I herewith lay before the Senate, for its constitutional action thereon, a treaty made on the 2d day of March, 1868, by and between Nathaniel G. Taylor, Commissioner of Indian Affairs; Alexander C. Hunt, governor andexofficiosuperintendent of Indian affairs of Colorado Territory, and Kit Carson, on the part of the United States, and the representatives of the Tabeguache, Muaehe, Capote, Weeminuche, Yampa, Grand River, and Uintah bands of Ute Indians.
A letter of the Secretary of the Interior of the 17th instant and the papers therein referred to are also herewith transmitted.
ANDREW JOHNSON.
