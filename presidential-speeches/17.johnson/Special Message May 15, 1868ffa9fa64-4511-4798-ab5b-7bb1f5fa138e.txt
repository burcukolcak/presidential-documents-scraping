Andrew Johnson
May 15, 1868

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit to the House of Representatives, in answer to their resolution of the 8th instant, a report++ from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers.
ANDREW JOHNSON.
++Relating to the detention, at the request of the House of Representatives, of the ironclad monitorsOneoto andCatawba, purchased from the United States by Swift & Co., and supposed to be intended for the Government of Peru, then at war with a power friendly to the United States.
