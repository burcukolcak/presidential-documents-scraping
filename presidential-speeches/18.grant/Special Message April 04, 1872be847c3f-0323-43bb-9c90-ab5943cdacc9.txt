Ulysses S. Grant
April 04, 1872

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 14th of January last, I transmit herewith a report* of the Secretary of State.
U. S. GRANT.
*Stating that the report of Richard D. Cutts on the marketable products of the sea was transmitted with the message of President Johnson of February 17, 1869.
