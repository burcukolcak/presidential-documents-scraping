Ulysses S. Grant
December 04, 1871

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In compliance with section 2 of the act making appropriations for the consular and diplomatic expenses of the Government for the year ending June 30, 1871, approved July 11, 1870, I herewith transmit the names and reports of and the amounts paid to consular agents of the United States.
U. S. GRANT.
