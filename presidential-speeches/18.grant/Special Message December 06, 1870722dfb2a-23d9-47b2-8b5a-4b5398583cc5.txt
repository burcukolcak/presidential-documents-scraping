Ulysses S. Grant
December 06, 1870

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I herewith transmit to Congress a report, dated the 5th instant, with the accompanying papers,* received from the Secretary of State, in compliance with the requirements of the eighteenth section of the act entitled "An act to regulate the diplomatic and consular systems of the United States," approvedAugust 18, 1856.
U. S. GRANT.
*Report of fees collected, etc., by consular officers of the United States for 1868, and tariff of consular fees prescribed by the President October 1, 1870.
