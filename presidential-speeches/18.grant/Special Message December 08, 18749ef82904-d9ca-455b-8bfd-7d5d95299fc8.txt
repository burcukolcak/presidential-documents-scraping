Ulysses S. Grant
December 08, 1874

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives.
I transmit herewith a report, dated the 8th instant, with accompanying papers,* from the Secretary of State, in compliance with the requirements of section 208 of the Revised Statutes of the United States.
U. S. GRANT.
*Report of fees collected, etc., by consular officers of the United States for 1873. list of consular officers, and tariff of consular fees prescribed by the President September 1, 1874.
