Ulysses S. Grant
December 08, 1874

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 3d of February, 1873, I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, together with the papers* which accompanied it.
U. S. GRANT.
*Dispatches in regard to the records and public documents of the Mexican Government relative to the lands embraced within the Territories of Arizona and New Mexico.
