Ulysses S. Grant
December 15, 1870

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 9th of April, 1869, I herewith transmit a report+ from the Secretary of State.
U. S. GRANT.
+Stating that all the correspondence relative to the condition of affairs in Paraguay believed to be required by the public interest had been made public.
