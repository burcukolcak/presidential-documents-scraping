Ulysses S. Grant
February 02, 1877

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit, in answer to a resolution of the Senate of the 10th ultimo, a report of the Secretary of State, with its accompanying papers.*
U. S. GRANT.
*Preliminary and final reports of J. Hubley Ashton, agent of the United States before the United States and Mexican Claims Commission.
