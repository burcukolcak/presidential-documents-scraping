Ulysses S. Grant
February 08, 1873

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 29th of January, requesting information in relation to the case of Bernhard Bernstein,* I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State upon that subject, with accompanying documents.
U. S. GRANT
*Claim against Russia for illegal arrest and imprisonment.
