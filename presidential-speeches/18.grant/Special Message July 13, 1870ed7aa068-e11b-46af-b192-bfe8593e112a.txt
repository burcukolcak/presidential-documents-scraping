Ulysses S. Grant
July 13, 1870

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit to the Senate, in answer to their resolution of the 8th instant, a report from the Secretary of State and the papers* which accompanied it.
U. S. GRANT.
*Instructions to the minister to Spain stating the basis on which the United States offered its good offices for the purpose of terminating the war in Cuba, correspondence relative thereto, etc.
