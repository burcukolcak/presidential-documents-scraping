Ulysses S. Grant
July 13, 1876

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith, in answer to a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 1st ultimo, a report+ from the Secretary of State upon the subject.
U.S. GRANT.
+Stating that no correspondence has taken place with Great Britain relative to the sequestration of the lands and property in New Zealand claimed by William Webster, an American citizen.
