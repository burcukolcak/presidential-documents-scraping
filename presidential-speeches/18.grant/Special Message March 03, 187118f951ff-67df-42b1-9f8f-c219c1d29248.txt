Ulysses S. Grant
March 03, 1871

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit to the Senate, in answer to their resolution of the 2d instant, report of the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents.*
U. S. GRANT.
* Correspondence from the United States legation at Constantinople relative to restrictions on the passage of the straits of the Dardanelles and the Bosphorus by the ships of other nations.
