Ulysses S. Grant
March 03, 1871

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit to the Senate, in answer to their resolution of February I, 1871, a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents.+
U. S. GRANT.
+ Dispatches, etc., from the United States minister to the Court of Brazil relative to the Paraguayan war, the culture of cotton in Brazil, trade with Brazil, etc.
