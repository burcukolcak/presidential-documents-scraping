Ulysses S. Grant
March 04, 1874

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I have the honor to transmit herewith replies from the several Departments, in answer to a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 16th of January last, requesting a list of all expenses incurred by the various Departments for transportation of any matter which before the abolition of the franking privilege was carried in the mails.
U. S. GRANT
