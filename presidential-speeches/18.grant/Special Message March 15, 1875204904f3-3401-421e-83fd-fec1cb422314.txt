Ulysses S. Grant
March 15, 1875

Special Message
To the Senate:
In answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 12th of March instant, I herewith transmit a report+ from the Secretary of State, with accompanying correspondence.
U. S. GRANT.
+Stating that the question of indemnity demanded from Spain for the execution or detention of a portion of the crew of the steamer "Virginius" and for the execution of passengers, citizens of the United States, had been disposed of by an agreement between the two countries, and transmitting correspondence connected therewith.
