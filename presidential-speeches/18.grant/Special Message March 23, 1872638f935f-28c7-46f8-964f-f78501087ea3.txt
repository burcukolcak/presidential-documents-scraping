Ulysses S. Grant
March 23, 1872

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In answer to a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 20th instant, I transmit a report from the Secretary of State, with a list of the newspapers++ which accompanied it.
U. S. GRANT.
++Selected to publish the laws of the United States for the second session of the Forty-second Congress.
