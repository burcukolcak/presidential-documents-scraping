Ulysses S. Grant
May 15, 1876

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 10th ultimo, I transmit herewith a report and accompanying papers upon the subject* from the Secretary of State.
U.S. GRANT.
*Course pursued to enforce the provisions of the convention with Venezuela of April 25, 1805, and the payment of adjudicated claims under act approved February 25, 1873.
