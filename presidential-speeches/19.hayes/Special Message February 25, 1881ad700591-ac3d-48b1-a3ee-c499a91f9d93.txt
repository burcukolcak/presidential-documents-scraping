Rutherford B. Hayes
February 25, 1881

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith, for the consideration of the Senate with a view to advising and consenting to the ratification thereof, a convention for the extradition of criminals, between the United States of America and the United States of Colombia, signed at Bogota on the 3d of January, 1881. I also transmit certain correspondence touching the negotiation of said convention.
R.B. HAYES
