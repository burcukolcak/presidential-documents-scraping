Rutherford B. Hayes
June 17, 1878

Special Message
To the Senate:
In answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 27th of May ultimo, I herewith transmit a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents.*
R.B. HAYES
*Correspondence, etc., relative to the selection of M. Maurice Delfosse as one of the commissioners under the treaty with Great Britain of May 8, 1871 on the fisheries question.
