Rutherford B. Hayes
May 14, 1878

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 29th ultimo, I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, with its accompanying papers.*
R.B. HAYES
*Correspondence relative to the terms and conditions under which the Cuban insurgents surrendered and to the policy of Spain in the government of Cuba.
