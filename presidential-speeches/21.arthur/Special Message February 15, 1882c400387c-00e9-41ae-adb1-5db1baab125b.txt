Chester A. Arthur
February 15, 1882

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith, for the information of Congress, the report of the Board of Indian Commissioners for the year 1881, accompanied by a letter from the Secretary of the Interior, dated the 9th instant, suggesting legislation regarding reports from said board.
The report is sent with the message to the House of Representatives.
CHESTER A. ARTHUR
