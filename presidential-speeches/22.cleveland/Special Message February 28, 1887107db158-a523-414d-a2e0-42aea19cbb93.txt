Grover Cleveland
February 28, 1887

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a communication of 17th instant from the Secretary of the Interior, submitting, with accompanying papers, two agreements made with Chippewa Indians in the State of Minnesota under the provisions of the act of May 15, 1886 (24 U. S. Statutes at Large, p. 44). The papers are presented for the consideration and action of Congress.
GROVER CLEVELAND
