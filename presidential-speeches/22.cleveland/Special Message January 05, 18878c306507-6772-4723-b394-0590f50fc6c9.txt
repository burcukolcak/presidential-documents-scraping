Grover Cleveland
January 05, 1887

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
Referring to my message of the 12th of January last, transmitting the final report of the commissioners appointed under the act of July 7, 1884, to visit the States of Central and South America, I have now to submit a special report by Commissioner Thomas C. Reynolds on the condition and commerce of Nicaragua, Honduras, and Salvador.
GROVER CLEVELAND
