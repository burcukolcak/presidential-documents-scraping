Grover Cleveland
January 15, 1889

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith, in response to the resolution of the Senate of the 4th instant, a report of the Secretary of State, with accompanying copies of correspondence, touching recent occurrences in the island of Hayti, both as relates to the state of the Government there and to the seizure and delivery up of the American vesselHaytien Republic.
GROVER CLEVELAND
