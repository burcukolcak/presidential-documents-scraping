Benjamin Harrison
March 11, 1889

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith, in answer to the Senate resolution of the 11th ultimo, a report of the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers, in regard to the case of Louis Riel, otherwise known as Louis David Riel.*
BENJ. HARRISON
*Tried and executed by the authorities of British North America for complicity in the rebellion in the Northwest Territory.
