Grover Cleveland
January 22, 1896

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In response to the resolution adopted by the Senate on December 1895, respecting what action had been taken in regard to the payment of the appropriation for the bounty on sugar contained in the sundry civil bill approved March 2, 1895, I herewith transmit a communication received from the Secretary of the Treasury, which contains all the information I have upon the subject.
GROVER CLEVELAND.
