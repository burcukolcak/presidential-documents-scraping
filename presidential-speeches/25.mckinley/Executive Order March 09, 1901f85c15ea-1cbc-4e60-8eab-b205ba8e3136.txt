William McKinley
March 09, 1901

Executive Order
Secretary of War:
Recommends modification of executive order of June 4, 1892, setting apart a wood reservation for the post of Fort Fill, Oklahoma Territory, so as to make the eastern boundary coincident with the new 98 meridian (the boundary line between the Kiowa and Comanche Reservation and the Chickasaw Nation) as serving a mark. pursuant to act of Congress of June 28, 1898 (30 Stars., 495).
