William McKinley
December 04, 1900

Message to Congress
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith, for the information of Congress, copy of a letter from the Commissioner-General of the United States to the Paris Exposition of 1900, of November 17, 1900, giving a detailed statement of the expenditures of the commission for the year ended November 15, 1900.
WILLIAM McKINLEY
