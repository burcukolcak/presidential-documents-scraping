Theodore Roosevelt
December 11, 1903

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State looking to the payment of this Government's quota of a fund to be contributed by the governments forming the International Union of American Republics for the erection of a building in the city of Washington for the use of the Bureau of the American Republics.
I approve the recommendations made by the Secretary of State, and strongly recommend the matter to the favorable consideration of Congress.
THEODORE ROOSEVELT.
