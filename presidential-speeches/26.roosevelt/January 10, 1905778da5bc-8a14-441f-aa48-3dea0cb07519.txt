Theodore Roosevelt
January 10, 1905

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a communication from the Secretary of the Interior relative to the reservation of certain lands in the abandoned Fort Sherman Military Reservation, in view of the contemplated use of such lands in connection with irrigation works to be constructed under the act of June 17, 1902. (32 Stat., 388.)
The matter is presented for the consideration of the Congress.
THEODORE ROOSEVELT.
