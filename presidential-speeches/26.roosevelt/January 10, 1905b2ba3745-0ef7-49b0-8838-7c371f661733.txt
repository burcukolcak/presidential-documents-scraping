Theodore Roosevelt
January 10, 1905

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith, for the consideration of the Congress, a communication from the Secretary of the Interior relative to the reestablishment of the boundary line between the State of Colorado and the Territories of New 'Mexico and Oklahoma, surveyed under authority of the act of Congress of July 1, 1902. (32 Stat., 552, 574.)
THEODORE ROOSEVELT.
