Theodore Roosevelt
March 02, 1905

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith, for the information of the Congress, a report on the Mexican cotton-boll weevil. Your attention is respectfully invited to the accompanying letter of the Secretary of Agriculture, recommending that at least 10,000 copies of this report be printed for the use of the Department of Agriculture, in addition to such number as may be desired for the use of the Senate and House of Representatives.
THEODORE ROOSEVELT.
