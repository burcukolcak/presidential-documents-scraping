William Howard Taft
November 05, 1912

Telegram to Woodrow Wilson Conceding the Presidential Election
Hon. Woodrow Wilson, Princeton, N.J.
I cordially congratulate you on your election, and extend to you my best wishes for a successful Administration.
WILLIAM H. TAFT.
Source: "Taft Owning Defeat, Calls to Deserters," New York Times, November 6, 1912, p. 5.
