Calvin Coolidge
August 12, 1924

Excerpts of the President's News Conference
I don't know whether I will take the report of the Tariff Commission on the sugar schedule to Vermont with me or not. It is quite a voluminous document, I find. I shall take it with me mentally.
Source: "The Talkative President: The Off-the-Record Press Conferences of Calvin Coolidge". eds. Howard H. Quint & Robert H. Ferrell. The University Massachusetts Press. 1964.
