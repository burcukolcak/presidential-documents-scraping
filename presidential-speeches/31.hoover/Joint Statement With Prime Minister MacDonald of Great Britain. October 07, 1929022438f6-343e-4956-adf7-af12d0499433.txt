Herbert Hoover
October 07, 1929

Joint Statement With Prime Minister MacDonald of Great Britain.
THE FOLLOWING joint statement was issued by the President and the Prime Minister today:
"We have frankly reviewed all the questions which might give rise to friction between our peoples. Gratifying progress has been made and conversations are continuing."
Note: Prime Minister MacDonald arrived in Washington on October 4. On the next day, he motored with the President to the Rapidan fishing camp where they held discussions until returning to Washington on October 7.
