Herbert Hoover
May 20, 1929

Letter Accepting Honorary Presidency of the International Longfellow Society.
[Released May 20, 1929. Dated May 15, 1929]
Dear Mr. Jackson:
I thank the International Longfellow Society most cordially for my election as Honorary President and accept with pleasure.
Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. Arthur Charles Jackson, President, The International Longfellow Society, 223 First Street, N.E., Washington, D.C. ]
