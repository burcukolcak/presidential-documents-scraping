Herbert Hoover
March 02, 1931

Message of Sympathy on the Death of Representative Henry Allen Cooper of Wisconsin.
My dear Mrs. Cooper:
I send you my deepest sympathy in your bereavement and I do pray that you may find consolation in the memory of his long and useful career. Congressman Cooper's courage and high ideals rendered him a truly valuable servant of our country.

Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mrs. Henry Allen Cooper, The Washington, Washington, D.C.]
Note: Representative Cooper served in the Congress from 1893 to 1919 and from 1921 to his death on March 1, 1931.
