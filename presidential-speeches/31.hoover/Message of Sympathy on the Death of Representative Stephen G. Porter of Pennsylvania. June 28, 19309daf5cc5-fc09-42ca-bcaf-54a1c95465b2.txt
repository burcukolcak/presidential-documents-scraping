Herbert Hoover
June 28, 1930

Message of Sympathy on the Death of Representative Stephen G. Porter of Pennsylvania.
[Released June 28, 1930. Dated June 27, 1930]
My dear Mrs. Baron:
I am shocked and grieved at the death of my friend, your father, Representative Stephen G. Porter, and I offer you my deepest sympathy in your bereavement. He will be long and gratefully remembered for his important services to the Nation, and I gladly bear witness to them of personal knowledge.

Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mrs. Richard F. Baron, 107 Centre Avenue, Ensworth, Pittsburgh, Pa.]
Note: Representative Porter served in the Congress from 1911 to his death.
