Herbert Hoover
October 06, 1931

Message of Sympathy on the Death of Senator Dwight W. Morrow.
I DEEPLY appreciate your wire advising me of the death of your brother. His passing is a shock to me and a loss to the world. Mrs. Hoover and I wish to express our heartfelt sympathy to you and yours.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Jay J. Morrow, Englewood, N.J.]
