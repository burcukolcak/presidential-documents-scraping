Herbert Hoover
July 25, 1930

Message of Sympathy to King Vittorio Emanuele III Following an Earthquake Disaster in Italy.
THE PEOPLE of the United States join me in extending to Your Majesty and to the people of Italy sincerest sympathy in the great losses suffered in the earthquake which has wrought such widespread destruction in the vicinity of Naples.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Majesty Vittorio Emanuele III, King of Italy, Rome]
Note: On July 24, 1930, an earthquake centering in the Naples area killed 1,778 people and injured 4,264.
