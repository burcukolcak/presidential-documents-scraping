Herbert Hoover
June 09, 1930

Message of Welcome to Julio Prestes, President-Elect of Brazil.
I HAVE just received your gracious message and hasten to assure you that I shall be indeed happy to welcome you to the United States where your visit is looked forward to with great pleasure by my countrymen and myself.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency, Dr. Julio Prestes, President-elect of Brazil, Steamship Almirante Jaceguay ]
