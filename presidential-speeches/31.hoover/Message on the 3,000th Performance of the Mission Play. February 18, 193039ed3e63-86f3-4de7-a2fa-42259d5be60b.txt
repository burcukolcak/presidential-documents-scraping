Herbert Hoover
February 18, 1930

Message on the 3,000th Performance of the Mission Play.
[Released February 18, 1930. Dated February 17, 1930]
I CONGRATULATE you cordially upon the 3,000th performance of the Mission Play which so vividly recreates the atmosphere of California's romantic early history.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. John S. McGroarty, c/o The Los Angeles Times, Los Angeles, California]
Note: The message was read at the 3,000th performance of the Mission Play, aproduction depiciting the development and decline of Spanish missions in colonial California. Mr. McGroarty was the author.
