Herbert Hoover
May 12, 1931

Message to King Carol II on Rumania's Independence Day.
[Released May 12, 1931. Dated May 11, 1931]
I TAKE PLEASURE in extending to Your Majesty and the Rumanian people felicitations on this auspicious anniversary of Rumanian independence.
HERBERT HOOVER
Note: As printed above, this item follows the text set forth in a contemporary news account.
