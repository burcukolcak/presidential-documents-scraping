Herbert Hoover
June 16, 1931

Message to King Gustaf V of Sweden on His Birthday.
[Released June 16, 1931. Dated June 15, 1931]
IT GIVES ME much pleasure on this anniversary of Your Majesty's birth to send you sincere felicitations and best wishes for a long and prosperous reign.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Majesty Gustaf V, King of Sweden, Stockholm, Sweden]
