Herbert Hoover
October 10, 1931

Message to President Chiang Chung-cheng on China's Independence Day.
I EXTEND to you and your fellow countrymen my sincere felicitations on this anniversary of the Republic of China.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Chiang Chung-cheng, President of the National Government of the Republic of China, Nanking, China]
Note: Chiang Chung-cheng was Chiang Kai-shek.
