Herbert Hoover
August 06, 1931

Message to President Daniel Salamanca on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Bolivian Republic.
ON BEHALF of the Government and people of the United States of America and in my own name I ask Your Excellency to accept hearty congratulations on this great national anniversary of Bolivia.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Daniel Salamanca, The President of Bolivia, La Paz, Bolivia]
