Herbert Hoover
November 03, 1930

Message to President Florencio H. Arosemena on Panama's Independence Day.
[Released November 3, 1930. Dated November 2, 1930]
ON THIS national holiday when Panama celebrates the anniversary of its independence, I take pleasure in sending to Your Excellency the sincere wishes of my Government, in which I personally join most heartily, for the increasing prosperity of your Nation.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Florencio H. Arosemena, The President of Panama, Panama City]
