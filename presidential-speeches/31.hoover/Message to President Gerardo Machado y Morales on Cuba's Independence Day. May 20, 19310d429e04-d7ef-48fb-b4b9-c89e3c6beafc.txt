Herbert Hoover
May 20, 1931

Message to President Gerardo Machado y Morales on Cuba's Independence Day.
IN THE NAME of the people of the United States, and in my own, I desire to extend to Your Excellency and to the people of Cuba on this memorable anniversary, most cordial greetings and best wishes.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency General Gerardo Machado y Morales, President of Cuba, Habana]
