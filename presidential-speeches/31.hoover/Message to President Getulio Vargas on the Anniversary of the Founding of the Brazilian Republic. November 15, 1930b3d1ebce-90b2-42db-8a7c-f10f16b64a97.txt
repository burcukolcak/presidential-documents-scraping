Herbert Hoover
November 15, 1930

Message to President Getulio Vargas on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Brazilian Republic.
IN THE NAME of the people of the United States and in my own, I desire to extend to Your Excellency and to the people of Brazil on this memorable anniversary most cordial greetings and best wishes for the continued prosperity of your great country.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Dr. Getulio Vargas, Provisional President of Brazil, Rio de Janeiro]
