Herbert Hoover
October 11, 1932

Message to President Lin Sen on China's Independence Day..
ON THIS ANNIVERSARY of the inauguration of the Chinese Republic, I send to Your Excellency sincere felicitations and best wishes both on behalf of the people of the United States and in my own name.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Lin Sen, President of the National Government of the Republic of China, Nanking, China]
