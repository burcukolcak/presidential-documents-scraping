Herbert Hoover
September 17, 1930

Message to President Pascual Ortiz Rubio on Mexico's Independence Day.
[Released September 17, 1930. Dated September 16, 1930]
MY FELLOW citizens join with me in extending cordial greetings on this Independence Day, and the assurances of our high regard and good wishes.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Pascual Ortiz Rubio, President of Mexico, Mexico City]
