Herbert Hoover
August 12, 1929

Message to President Paul von Hindenburg on the Anniversary of the Founding of the German Republic.
[Released August 12, 1929. Dated August 11, 1929]
I BEG of Your Excellency to accept my heartiest congratulations on this anniversary of the founding of the German Republic.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency President von Hindenburg, Berlin, Germany]
