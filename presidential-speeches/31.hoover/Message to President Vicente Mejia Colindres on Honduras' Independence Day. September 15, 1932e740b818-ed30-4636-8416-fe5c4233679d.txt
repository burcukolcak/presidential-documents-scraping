Herbert Hoover
September 15, 1932

Message to President Vicente Mejia Colindres on Honduras' Independence Day.
ON THIS memorable fifteenth of September, I offer to Your Excellency my sincere congratulations and extend my best wishes for the happiness and prosperity of your fellow countrymen.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Vicente Mejia Colindres, The President of Honduras, Tegucigalpa, Honduras]
