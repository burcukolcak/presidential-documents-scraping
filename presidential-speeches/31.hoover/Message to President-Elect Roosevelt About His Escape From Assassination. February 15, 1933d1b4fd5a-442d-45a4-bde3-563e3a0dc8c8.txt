Herbert Hoover
February 15, 1933

Message to President-Elect Roosevelt About His Escape From Assassination.
TOGETHER with every citizen I rejoice that you have not been injured. I shall be grateful to you for news of Mayor Cermak's condition.
[President-elect Franklin D. Roosevelt, Miami, Fla.]
Note: On February 15, 1933, in Miami, Fla., President-elect Roosevelt and his party were shot at 6 times by Giuseppe Zangara, an unemployed bricklayer. President-elect Roosevelt was not injured, but several others were wounded, including Mayor Anton Cermak of Chicago, who died on March 6.
