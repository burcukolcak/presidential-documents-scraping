Herbert Hoover
June 17, 1929

Message to the First Meeting of the International Hospital Congress.
[Released June 17, 1929. Dated June 13, 1929]
PLEASE EXPRESS to the International Hospital Congress my cordial greetings and my best wishes for a most valuable interchange of ideas upon the vitally important subject of your gathering.
Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
Note: The message was sent to Mr. Rene Sand, president, International Hospital Congress, Hotel Ritz, Atlantic City, N.J.
