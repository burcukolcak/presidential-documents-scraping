Herbert Hoover
September 06, 1932

Message to the Lafayette-Marne Day Committee.
[Released September 6, 1932. Dated September 1, 1932]
THE COINCIDENCE of Lafayette's birthday and the anniversary of the battle of the Marne annually evokes in all American hearts a warm response of sentiment to these two names, each of which is a symbol of liberty.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. Maurice Leon, 44 Wall Street, New York City]
Note: The Marquis de Lafayette, a French officer who served during the American Revolutionary War, was born on September 6, 1757.
The first Battle of the Marne began on September 6, 1914, in France, during World War I.
