Herbert Hoover
May 07, 1932

Message to the Princeton Alumni Weekly Honoring John Grief Hibben.
[Released May 7, 1932. Dated February 24, 1932]
PRESIDENT John Grier Hibben's distinguished service in the cause of education will long remain in the memory of the Nation and in the gratitude of those whose lives he has helped to direct and enrich.
Note: The message was printed in a special issue of the Princeton Alumni Weekly. The issue was devoted to the career of Dr. Hibben, president of Princeton University from 1912 until his retirement in June 1932.
