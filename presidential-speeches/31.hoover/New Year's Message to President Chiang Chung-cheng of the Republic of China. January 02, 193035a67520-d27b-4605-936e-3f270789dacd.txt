Herbert Hoover
January 02, 1930

New Year's Message to President Chiang Chung-cheng of the Republic of China.
[Released January 2, 1930. Dated December 28, 1929]
I THANK Your Excellency in my own name and on behalf of my fellow countrymen for your courteous new year greetings which are most heartily reciprocated.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Chiang Chung-cheng, The President of the Chinese Republic, Nanking, China]
