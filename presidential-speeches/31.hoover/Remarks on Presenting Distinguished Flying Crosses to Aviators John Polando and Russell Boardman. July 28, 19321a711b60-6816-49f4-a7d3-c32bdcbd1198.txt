Herbert Hoover
July 28, 1932

Remarks on Presenting Distinguished Flying Crosses to Aviators John Polando and Russell Boardman.
I AM GLAD to greet you and express my appreciation and the admiration of the whole American people for your daring exploit.
Note: The President spoke at 12:30 p.m. on the White House grounds.
As printed above, this item follows the text set forth in a contemporary news account.
Boardman and Polando set a long distance record by flying a Bellanca monoplane, the Cape Cod, nonstop from New York to Istanbul in 49 hours and 20 minutes.
