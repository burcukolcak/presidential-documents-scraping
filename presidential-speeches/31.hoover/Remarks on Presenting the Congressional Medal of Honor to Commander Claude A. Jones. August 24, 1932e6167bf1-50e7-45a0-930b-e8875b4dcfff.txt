Herbert Hoover
August 24, 1932

Remarks on Presenting the Congressional Medal of Honor to Commander Claude A. Jones.
IT GIVES ME great pleasure to present to you this medal in recognition of your courageous and highly commendable action.
Note: The President spoke at presentation ceremonies at the White House. Commander Jones was decorated for extraordinary heroism during a hurricane off Santo Domingo on August 29, 1916.
As printed above, this item follows the text of a contemporary news account.
