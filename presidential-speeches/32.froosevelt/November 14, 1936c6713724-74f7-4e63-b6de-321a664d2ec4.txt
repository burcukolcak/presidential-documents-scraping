EXECUTIVE ORDER 7495
DESIGNATING ORANGE, TEXAS, AS A CUSTOMS PORT OF ENTRY
November 14, 1936
By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (U.S.C., title 19, sec. 2), I hereby designate Orange, Texas, as a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 21 (Sabine), effective this date.
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

THE WHITE HOUSE,

November 14, 1936.
Exec. Order No. 7495, 1 FR 2161, 1936 WL 2845 (Pres.)
