Dwight D. Eisenhower
August 13, 1955

Exchange of Messages Between the President and Chancellor Adenauer on the Air Force Disaster in Germany.
[Released August 13, 1955. Dated August 12, 1955]
I HAVE RECEIVED your expression of regret concerning the Air Force disaster in Germany. I am most grateful for your sympathy and have made your kind message known to the American people.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
Note: Chancellor Adenaucr's message, received by the Secretary of State, follows:
On the occasion of the serious disaster which occurred to the United States Air Force by the crashing of the troop transport near Edelweiler, I would like you to transmit to the President of the United States my deepest sympathy. I express my sincere condolences to the relatives of the soldiers who met with the fatal accident.
