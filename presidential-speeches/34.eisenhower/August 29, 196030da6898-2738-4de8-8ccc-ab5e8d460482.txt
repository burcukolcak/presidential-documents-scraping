Dwight D. Eisenhower
August 29, 1960

Message to Prime Minister Chang Myon of Korea.
Dear Mr. Prime Minister:
Please accept my congratulations and warm good wishes upon your accession to the post of Prime Minister of the Republic of Korea. This honor is fitting recognition of your record of distinguished service to your country.
I remember with pleasure our enjoyable and useful conversation at breakfast during my visit to Seoul, and I wish you every success in carrying out the heavy burden of your office and in fulfilling the trust which has been placed in your hands.

Sincerely,
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
