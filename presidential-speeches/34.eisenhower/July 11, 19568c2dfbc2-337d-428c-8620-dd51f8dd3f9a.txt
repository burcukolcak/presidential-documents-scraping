Dwight D. Eisenhower
July 11, 1956

Message to the King of Greece Conveying Sympathy Following an Earthquake Disaster.
His Majesty

Paul I

King of the Hellenes
I am saddened to learn that Greece has just suffered an earthquake disaster and I wish to convey the heartfelt sympathy of the American people to you and to the people of the Islands.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
NOTE.: This message was released at Gettysburg, Pa.
