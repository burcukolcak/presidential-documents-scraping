Dwight D. Eisenhower
September 16, 1955

Message to President Ruiz Cortines on the Anniversary of the Independence of Mexico.
His Excellency

Senor Don Adolfo Ruiz Cortines

President of the United Mexican States
It gives me pleasure on this anniversary of the Independence of the United Mexican States to convey to Your Excellency and to the people of Mexico my sincere good wishes and felicitations as well as those of the people of the United States.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
Note: This message was released at Lowry Air Force Base, Denver, Colo.
