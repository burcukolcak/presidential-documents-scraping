Dwight D. Eisenhower
September 26, 1959

Message to the Governor General of Ceylon on the Death of Prime Minister Bandaranaike.
His Excellency

Sir Oliver Ernest Goonetilleke,

G.C.M.G., K.C.V.O., K.B.E.

Governor General of Ceylon
Dear Governor General:
I was deeply shocked to hear of the passing of Prime Minister Bandaranaike. The world has lost a true friend of peace. Please extend my deepest sympathies to Mrs. Bandaranaike and to the people of Ceylon.

Sincerely,
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
Note: This message was released at Gettysburg, Pa.
