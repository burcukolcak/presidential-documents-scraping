John F. Kennedy
October 05, 1962

Joint Statement Following Discussions With Crown Prince Faysal of Saudi Arabia.
ON OCTOBER 5 His Royal Highness Crown Prince Faysal and President Kennedy held private talks at the White House. Frank and cordial discussions were held on Saudi Arabian-American relations and on the world situation. Crown Prince Faysal and the President are confident that this opportunity to become personally acquainted will lead to increased mutual understanding between the United States and Saudi Arabia.
