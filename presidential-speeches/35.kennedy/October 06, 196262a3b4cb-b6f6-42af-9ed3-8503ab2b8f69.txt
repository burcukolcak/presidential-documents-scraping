John F. Kennedy
October 06, 1962

Reply to Chairman Khrushchev's Message on the Flight of Commander Schirra.
[Released October 6, 1962. Dated October 5, 1962]
Dear Mr. Chairman:
I thank you and the Soviet people on behalf of the American people for your congratulations on Commander Schirra's successful space flight. Your greetings and best wishes have been conveyed to Commander Schirra.
JOHN F. KENNEDY
Note: Chairman Khrushchev's letter, dated October 4, was released in Detroit with the President's reply.
