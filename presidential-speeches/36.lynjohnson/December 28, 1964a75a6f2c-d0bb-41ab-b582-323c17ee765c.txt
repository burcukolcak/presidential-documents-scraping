Lyndon B. Johnson
December 28, 1964

Message to Giuseppe Saragat Upon His Election as President of Italy.
ON BEHALF of the American people, I extend to you warm congratulations upon your election as President. I look forward to a continuation of the close friendship between our two countries in these times which present not only great problems but also great opportunities. Mrs. Johnson joins me in sending our personal best wishes.

Sincerely,
LYNDON B. JOHNSON
[His Excellency Giuseppe Saragat, Rome, Italy]
Note: The message was released at Austin, Tex.
