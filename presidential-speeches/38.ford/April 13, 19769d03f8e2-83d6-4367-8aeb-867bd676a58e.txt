Gerald R. Ford
April 13, 1976

Special Message to the Congress Reporting on a Budget Deferral
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I report an increase of $3.9 million in the amount previously deferred for the Department of the Interior's program to develop roads and trails on public lands.

The details of the revised deferral are contained in the attached report.
GERALD R. FORD

The White House,

April 13, 1976.
Note: The attachment detailing the deferral is printed in the Federal Register of April 16, 1976 (41 FR 16434).
