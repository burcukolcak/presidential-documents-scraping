Gerald R. Ford
March 28, 1975

Message to the Congress Transmitting Annual Report on the Status of Federal Advisory Committees.
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the provisions of section 6(c) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the report on the status of advisory committees in 1974 is herewith forwarded.
This is the third annual report. It is organized to improve public access to information concerning specific advisory committees and the Federal agencies to whom they provide advice.
GERALD R. FORD

The White House,

March 28, 1975.
Note: The report is entitled "Federal Advisory Committees--Third Annual Report of the President, Covering Calendar Year 1974" (Government Printing Office, 150 pp.).
