Jimmy Carter
July 30, 1980

Budget Deferrals Message to the Congress. 
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report two revisions to previously transmitted deferrals increasing the amount deferred by $137 thousand. These items affect programs in the Department of Defense and the International Communication Agency.
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
JIMMY CARTER

The White House,

July 30, 1980.
Note: The attachments detailing the deferrals are printed in the FEDERAL REGISTER of August 1, 1980.
