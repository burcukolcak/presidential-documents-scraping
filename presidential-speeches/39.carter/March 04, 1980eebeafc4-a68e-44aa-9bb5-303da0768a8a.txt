Jimmy Carter
March 04, 1980

Budget Rescission Message to the Congress. 
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report a proposal to rescind $17.0 million in funds appropriated for atomic energy defense activities in the Department of Energy. The details of this rescission proposal are contained in the attached report.
JIMMY CARTER

The White House,

March 4, 1980.
Note: The attachment detailing the rescission is printed in the FEDERAL REGISTER Of March 10, 1980.
