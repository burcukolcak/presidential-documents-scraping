Ronald Reagan
August 07, 1985

Message to Hugh Desmond Hoyte on the Death of President Linden Forbes Sampson Burnham of Guyana 
Dear Mr. President:
It was with sadness that I learned of the unexpected death of President Linden Forbes Sampson Burnham. Please convey my sincere condolences to President Burnham's family and to the people of Guyana at this time of grief.

Sincerely,
RONALD REAGAN
Note: First Vice President Hoyte succeeded to the Presidency on the death of President Burnham.
