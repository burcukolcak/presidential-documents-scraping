Ronald Reagan
September 23, 1982

Message to the Congress Reporting Budget Deferrals 
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report a new deferral of $6,500,000 for the Department of Commerce and a revision to an existing deferral increasing the amount deferred by $1,250,000 for the United States Information Agency (formerly the International Communication Agency).
The details of the deferrals are contained in the attached reports.
RONALD REAGAN

The White House,

September 23, 1982.
Note: The attachments detailing the deferrals are printed in the Federal Register of September 29, 1982.
