George Bush
January 03, 1990

Letter to Congressional Leaders on Multilateral Loans to China
Dear XXXXX:
As required by Title I of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1990, I have determined that the International Development Association has not provided any new loans to China since June 27, 1989.
This determination, a copy of which is enclosed, shall be published in the Federal Register.
Sincerely,
George Bush
Note: The letter was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on January 4.
