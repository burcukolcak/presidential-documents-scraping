William J. Clinton
April 13, 1993

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Report on the North Atlantic Treaty
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
As requested in section 1314 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1993 (Public Law 102-484), I am forwarding the "Report on the North Atlantic Treaty of 1949."
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
