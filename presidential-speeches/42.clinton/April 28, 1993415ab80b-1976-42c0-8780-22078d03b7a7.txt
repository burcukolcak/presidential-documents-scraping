William J. Clinton
April 28, 1993

Exchange With Reporters Prior to a Meeting With National Governors' Association Representatives on Health Care Reform
Q. Mr. President, do you feel that you can extend health care to all the uninsured in the first—
The President. I think we're going to have a very successful health care program that will be very much like the one I talked about when I started the campaign. It will be very good. I think—
Q. But do you think you can do it?
The President. Wait until we come out with it; you'll see.
NOTE: The exchange began at 4:37 p.m. in the State Dining Room at the White House. A tape was not available for verification of the content of this exchange.
