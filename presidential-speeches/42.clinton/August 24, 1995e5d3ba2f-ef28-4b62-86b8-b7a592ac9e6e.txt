William J. Clinton
August 24, 1995

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Haiti
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
As required by section 107 of Public Law 104-6, I transmit herewith the report on the cumulative incremental cost of all United States activities in Haiti subsequent to September 30, 1993.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Newt Gingrich, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
