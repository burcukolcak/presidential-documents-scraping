William J. Clinton
December 01, 1995

Exchange With Reporters Prior to Discussions With President Mary Robinson of Ireland in Dublin
President's Visit
Q.Mr. President, how did you like the reception when you came in?
The President.I liked it very well. I was delighted to see the people in the streets and delighted to be with President Robinson again.
Q.What's on the agenda for the discussions this morning?
The President.More of the same. [Laughter]
Bosnia
Q.How do you like Senator Dole's support of Bosnia?
The President.I'm very gratified by it. I appreciate it very much.
NOTE: The exchange began at 11:07 a.m. at Arasan Uachtarain, President Robinson's residence. A tape was not available for verification of the content of this exchange.
