William J. Clinton
December 27, 1993

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Implementation of NAFTA
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
Pursuant to Section 101(b)(1)(B) of the North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act, I am pleased to submit the attached report regarding the implementation of the North American Free Trade Agreement.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate. This letter was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on December 28.
