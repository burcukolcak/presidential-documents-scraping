William J. Clinton
February 28, 1998

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Trade Policy Agenda and Trade Agreements Program Report
To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 163 of the Trade Act of 1974, as amended (19 U.S.C 2213), I transmit herewith the 1998 Trade Policy Agenda and 1997 Annual Report on the Trade Agreements Program.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, February 28, 1998.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on March 2.
