William J. Clinton
January 03, 1994

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Loan Guarantees to Israel
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
Enclosed is an unclassified report on the Loan Guarantees to Israel Program as required by section 226(k) of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended (Public Law 87-195).
I hope this report will be useful to you.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
