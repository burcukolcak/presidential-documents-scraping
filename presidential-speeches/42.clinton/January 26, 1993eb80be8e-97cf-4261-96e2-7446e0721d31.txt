William J. Clinton
January 26, 1993

Exchange With Reporters Prior to a Meeting with Congressional Leaders
Q. Mr. President, are you going to have a policy statement on gays in the military by the end of the week?
The President. I'm going to talk to the leadership of Congress about our legislative agenda.
Q. How do you feel your meeting with the Joint Chiefs went yesterday, Mr. President?
The President. It was a very good meeting. I was very impressed by them. The country's well served.
NOTE: The President spoke at 9:38 a.m. in the Cabinet Room at the White House.
