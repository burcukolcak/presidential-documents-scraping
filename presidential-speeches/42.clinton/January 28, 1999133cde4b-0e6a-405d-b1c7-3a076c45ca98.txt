William J. Clinton
January 28, 1999

Memorandum on Funding for International Financial Institutions and Other International Organizations and Programs
Presidential Determination No. 99-11
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject:Presidential Determination Pursuant to Section 523 of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1999 (as contained in the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1999, Public Law 105-277)
Pursuant to section 523 of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 1999 (as contained in the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1999, Public Law 105-277), I hereby certify that withholding from international financial institutions and other international organizations and programs funds appropriated or otherwise made available pursuant to that Act is contrary to the national interest.
You are authorized and directed to publish this determination in theFederal Register.
William J. Clinton
Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on January 29.
