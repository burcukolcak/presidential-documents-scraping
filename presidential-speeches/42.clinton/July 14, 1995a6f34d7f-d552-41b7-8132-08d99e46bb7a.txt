William J. Clinton
July 14, 1995

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Revision to the United States Arctic Research Plan
To the Congress of the United States:
Pursuant to the provisions of the Arctic Research and Policy Act of 1984, as amended (15 U.S.C. 4108(a)), I transmit herewith the fourth biennial revision (1996-2000) to the United States Arctic Research Plan.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, July 14, 1995.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on July 17.
