William J. Clinton
June 30, 1994

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Treasury Department Report
Dear Mr. Chairman:
As required by section 511 of the Foreign Relations Authorization Act, FY 94-95 (Public Law 103-236), I hereby transmit the report prepared by the Treasury Department on expenditures from blocked accounts.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Lee H. Hamilton, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs, and Claiborne Pell, chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations.
