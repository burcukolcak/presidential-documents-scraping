William J. Clinton
March 27, 1995

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report on the Health Care for Native Hawaiians Program
To the Congress of the United States:
I transmit herewith the Report on the Health Care for Native Hawaiians Program, as required by section 11 of the Native Hawaiians Health Care Act of 1988, as amended (Public Law 102-396; 42 U.S.C. 11701et seq.).
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, March 27, 1995.
