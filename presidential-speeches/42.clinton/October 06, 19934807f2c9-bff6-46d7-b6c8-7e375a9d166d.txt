William J. Clinton
October 06, 1993

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the National Corporation for Housing Partnerships
To the Congress of the United States:
I transmit herewith the twenty-fourth annual report of the National Corporation for Housing Partnerships and the National Housing Partnership for the fiscal year ending December 31, 1992, as required by section 3938(a)(1) of title 42 of the United States Code.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, October 6, 1993.
