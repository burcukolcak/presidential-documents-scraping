William J. Clinton
October 06, 1994

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Railroad Retirement Board Report
To the Congress of the United States:
I hereby submit to the Congress the Annual Report of the Railroad Retirement Board for Fiscal Year 1993, pursuant to the provisions of section 7(b)(6) of the Railroad Retirement Act and section 12(1) of the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, October 6, 1994.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on October 7.
