William J. Clinton
October 18, 1994

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting an Immigration Report
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
I transmit herewith the President's Report on Immigration. This interim report is required by section 141(i) of the Immigration Act of 1990, as amended (Public Law 101-649).
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
