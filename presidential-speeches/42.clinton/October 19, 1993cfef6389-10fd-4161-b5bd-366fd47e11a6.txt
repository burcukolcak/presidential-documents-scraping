William J. Clinton
October 19, 1993

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with section 5347(e) of title 5 of the United States Code, I transmit herewith the 1992 annual report of the Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, October 19, 1993.
