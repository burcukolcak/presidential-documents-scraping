William J. Clinton
September 20, 2000

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Report of the Trade and Development Agency
Dear Mr. Chairman:
As required by section 201(d) of the Jobs Through Exports Act of 1992 (Public Law 102- 549; 22 U.S.C. 2421(d)), I transmit herewith the annual report of the Trade and Development Agency for fiscal year 1999.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Jesse Helms, chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; and Benjamin A. Gilman, chairman, House Committee on International Relations.
