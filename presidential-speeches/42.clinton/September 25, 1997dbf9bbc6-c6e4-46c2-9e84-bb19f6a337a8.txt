William J. Clinton
September 25, 1997

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Future Free Trade Area Negotiations
Dear Mr. Chairman:
In accordance with section 108(b)(4) of the North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act (19 U.S.C. 3317(b)(4)), I transmit herewith the report containing recommendations on future free trade area negotiations.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Bill Archer, chairman, House Committee on Ways and Means, and William V. Roth, Jr., chairman, Senate Finance Committee.
