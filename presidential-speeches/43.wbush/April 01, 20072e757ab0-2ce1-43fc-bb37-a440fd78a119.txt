George W. Bush
April 01, 2007

Notice - Notice of Intention To Enter Into a Free Trade Agreement With Korea
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of the Trade Act of 2002, I have notified the Congress of my intention to enter into a free trade agreement with the Republic of Korea.
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of that Act, this notice shall be published in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush

The White House,

April 1, 2007.
Note: This notice was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on April 2, and it was published in the Federal Register on April 3.
