George W. Bush
December 21, 2004

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Implementation of Debt Reduction Authority
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
Consistent with section 1321 of the Foreign Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Year 2003 (Public Law 107-228), I transmit herewith a report prepared by my Administration on implementation of the debt reduction authority conferred by Title XIII, Subtitle B of Public Law 107-228.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Richard B. Cheney, President of the Senate.
