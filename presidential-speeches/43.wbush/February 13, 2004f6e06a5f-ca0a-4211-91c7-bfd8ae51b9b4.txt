George W. Bush
February 13, 2004

Notice - Notice of Intention To Enter Into a Free Trade Agreement With Australia
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of the Trade Act of 2002, I have notified the Congress of my intention to enter into a free trade agreement with the Government of Australia.
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of that Act, this notice shall be published in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush

The White House,

February 13, 2004. [Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 11:55 a.m., February 17, 2004]
Note: This notice was published in the Federal Register on February 18. This item was not received in time for publication in the appropriate issue.
