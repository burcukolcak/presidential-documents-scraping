George W. Bush
February 26, 2001

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on the Korean Peninsula Energy Development Organization
Dear Mr. Chairman:
I transmit herewith the 6-month report required under the heading "International Organizations and Programs" in title IV of the Foreign Operations Appropriations Act, 1996 (Public Law 104-107), relating to the Korean Peninsula Energy Development Organization. The report, which was prepared by the previous Administration, covers the period from August 13, 2000, through January 27, 2001.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Ted Stevens, chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations, and C.W. Bill Young, chairman, House Committee on Appropriations.
