George W. Bush
July 22, 2003

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Progress Report on Spending in Support of Plan Colombia
To the Congress of the United States:
Consistent with section 3204(e), Public Law 106-246, I am providing a report prepared by my Administration detailing the progress of spending by the executive branch during the first two quarters of Fiscal Year 2003 in support of Plan Colombia.
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, July 22, 2003.
