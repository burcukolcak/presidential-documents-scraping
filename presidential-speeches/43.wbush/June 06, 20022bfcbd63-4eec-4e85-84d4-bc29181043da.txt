George W. Bush
June 06, 2002

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the Corporation for Public Broadcasting
To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 19(3) of the Public Telecommunications Act of 1992 (Law 102-356), I transmit herewith the report of the Corporation for Public Broadcasting for calendar year 2001.
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, June 6, 2002.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on June 11. An original was not available for verification of the content of this message.
