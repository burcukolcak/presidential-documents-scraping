George W. Bush
June 11, 2001

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the National Endowment for Democracy
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the provisions of section 504(h) of Public Law 98-164, as amended (22 U.S.C. 4413(i)), I transmit herewith the Annual Report of the National Endowment for Democracy for fiscal year 2000.
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, June 11, 2001.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on June 13.
