George W. Bush
March 01, 2004

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on International Agreements
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. Chairman:)
Consistent with subsection (b) of the Case-Zablocki Act, (1 U.S.C. 112b), I hereby transmit a report prepared by the Department of State concerning international agreements.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Richard G. Lugar, chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations.
