George W. Bush
March 30, 2007

Notice - Intention To Enter Into a Free Trade Agreement With Panama
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of the Trade Act of 2002, I have notified the Congress of my intention to enter into a free trade agreement with the Republic of Panama.
Consistent with section 2105(a)(1)(A) of that Act, this notice shall be published in the Federal Register.
George W. Bush

The White House,

March 30, 2007.
Note: This notice was published in the Federal Register on April 2.
