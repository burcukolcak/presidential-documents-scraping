Barack Obama
June 08, 2009

Memorandum on Delegation of Certain Functions Under Section 201 of Public Law 110-429
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject:Delegation of Certain Functions Under Section 201 of Public Law 110-429
By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, including section 301 of title 3, United States Code, I hereby delegate to you all functions conferred upon the President by subsections (a), (b), and (c) of section 201 of Public Law 110-429. You will exercise these functions in coordination with the Secretary of Defense.
You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in theFederal Register.
BARACK OBAMA
NOTE: This memorandum was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on June 9.
