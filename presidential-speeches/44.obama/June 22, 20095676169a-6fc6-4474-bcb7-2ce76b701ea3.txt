Barack Obama
June 22, 2009

Statement on the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority Commuter Train Crash in Washington, DC
Michelle and I were saddened by the terrible accident in northeast Washington, DC, today. Our thoughts and prayers go out to the families and friends affected by this tragedy. I want to thank the brave first responders who arrived immediately to save lives. My staff has been in touch with Mayor Fenty's office and will continue to monitor the situation.
Note: The statement referred to Mayor Adrian M. Fenty of Washington, DC.
