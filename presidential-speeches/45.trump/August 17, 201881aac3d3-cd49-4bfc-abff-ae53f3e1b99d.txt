Donald J. Trump
August 17, 2018

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on the Accession of Macedonia to the North Atlantic Treaty
Dear Mr. Chairman:
I am providing the Macedonia NATO Accession Report, prepared consistent with section 3(2)(E)(i) of the Resolution of Advice and Consent to Ratification of the Protocols to the North Atlantic Treaty of 1949 on the Accession of Poland, Hungary, and the Czech Republic of April 30, 1998.
Sincerely,
DONALD J. TRUMP
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Richard C. Shelby, chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations; John S. McCain III, chairman, Senate Committee on Armed Services; Robert P. Corker, Jr., chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; Richard M. Burr, chairman, Senate Select Committee on Intelligence; Rodney P. Frelinghuysen, chairman, House Committee on Appropriations; W. McClellan "Mac" Thornberry, chairman, House Committee on Armed Services; and Edward R. Royce, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs.
