Donald J. Trump
December 14, 2018

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Security and Stability Strategy for Somalia
Dear Mr. Chairman:
In accordance with section 1271 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (Public Law 115-91), I transmit herewith a security and stability strategy for Somalia.
Sincerely,
DONALD J. TRUMP
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Richard C. Shelby, chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations; James M. Inhofe, chairman, Senate Committee on Armed Services; Robert P. Corker, Jr., chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; Richard M. Burr, chairman, Senate Select Committee on Intelligence; Rodney P. Frelinghuysen, chairman, House Committee on Appropriations; W. McClellan "Mac" Thornberry, chairman, House Committee on Armed Services; Edward R. Royce, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs; and Devin G. Nunes, chairman, House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence.
