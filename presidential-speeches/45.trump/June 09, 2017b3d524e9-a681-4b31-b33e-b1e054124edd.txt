Donald J. Trump
June 09, 2017

Remarks Prior to a Meeting with President Klaus Iohannis of Romania and an Exchange With Reporters
President Trump.Well, thank you very much. It's a great honor to have you—been a true ally, wonderful country, and a great relationship. And that relationship, as you know, as of moments ago, is getting even better. So thank you very much for being here. It's my honor.
President Iohannis.Thank you very much, Mr. President. It's a great pleasure and honor for me to be here, and I'm looking forward to our discussions and our partnership.
President Trump.Yes, it's been a great partnership.
Thank you all very much. Thank you.
