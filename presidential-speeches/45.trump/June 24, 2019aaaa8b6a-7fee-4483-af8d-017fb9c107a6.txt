Donald J. Trump
June 24, 2019

Message to the Congress on Imposing Sanctions With Respect to Iran
To the Congress of the United States:
Pursuant to the International Emergency Economic Powers Act (50 U.S.C. 1701et seq.) (IEEPA), I hereby report that I have issued an Executive Order with respect to Iran that takes additional steps with respect to the national emergency declared in Executive Order 12957 of March 15, 1995.
I am enclosing a copy of the order I have issued.
DONALD J. TRUMP
The White House,
June 24, 2019.
