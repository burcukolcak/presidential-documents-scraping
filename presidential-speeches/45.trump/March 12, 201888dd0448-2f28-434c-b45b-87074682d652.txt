Donald J. Trump
March 12, 2018

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Legal and Policy Frameworks Guiding the United States Use of Military Force and Related National Security Operations
Dear Chairman:
In accordance with section 1264 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (Public Law 115-91), I transmit herewith the attached report on Legal and Policy Frameworks Guiding the United States Use of Military Force and Related National Security Operations.
Sincerely,

DONALD J. TRUMP
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to W. Thad Cochran, chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations; John S. McCain III, chairman, Senate Committee on Armed Services; Robert P. Corker, Jr., chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; Richard M. Burr, chairman, Senate Select Committee on Intelligence; Rodney P. Frelinghuysen, chairman, House Committee on Appropriations; William "Mac" Thornberry, chairman, House Committee on Armed Services; Edward R. Royce, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs; and Devin G. Nunes, chairman, House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence.
