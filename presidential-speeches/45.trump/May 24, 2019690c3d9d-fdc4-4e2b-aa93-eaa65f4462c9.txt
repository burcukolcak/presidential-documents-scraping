Donald J. Trump
May 24, 2019

Memorandum on Revisions to the 2017 Unified Command Plan
Memorandum for the Secretary of Defense
Subject:Revisions to the 2017 Unified Command Plan
Pursuant to my authority as Commander in Chief, I hereby approve and direct the implementation of the revised Unified Command Plan.
Consistent with section 161(b)(2) of title 10, United States Code, and section 301 of title 3, United States Code, you are directed to notify the Congress on my behalf.
You are authorized and directed to publish this memorandum in theFederal Register.
DONALD J. TRUMP
NOTE: This memorandum was published in the Federal Register on May 30.
