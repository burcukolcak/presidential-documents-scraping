Donald J. Trump
November 26, 2018

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Classified Version of the National Strategy for Counterterrorism
Dear Mr. Chairman:
In accordance with section 1094(a) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (Public Law 115-91), I have enclosed the classified version of the National Strategy for Counterterrorism.
Sincerely,
DONALD J. TRUMP
