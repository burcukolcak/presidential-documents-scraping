Donald J. Trump
September 12, 2019

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report in Accordance With the Elie Wiesel Genocide and Atrocities Prevention Act of 2018
Dear Mr. Chairman: (Dear Madam Chairwoman:)
In accordance with section 5 of the Elie Wiesel Genocide and Atrocities Prevention Act of 2018 (Public Law 115-441), I transmit herewith a report that includes a review of current efforts to prevent and respond to atrocities, a review of ongoing global atrocities, and recommendations to prevent and respond to atrocities produced in consultation with civil society and academic and other nongovernmental organizations and institutions.
Sincerely,
DONALD J. TRUMP
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Richard C. Shelby, chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations; James E. Risch, chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; Nita N. Lowey, chairwoman, House Committee on Appropriations; and Eliot L. Engel, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs. An original was not available for verification of the content of this letter.
