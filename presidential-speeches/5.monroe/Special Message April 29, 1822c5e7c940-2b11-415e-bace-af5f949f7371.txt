James Monroe
April 29, 1822

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit to the House of Representatives a report from the Secretary of State, in pursuance of their resolution of the 29th instant*,(An error; so in the original message. The date of the resolution is the 18th of April,) "requesting to be furnished with a copy of the judicial proceedings in the United States court for the district of Louisiana in the case of the French slave shipLa Pensee."
JAMES MONROE.
