John Quincy Adams
February 20, 1829

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the Senate of the 10th instant, requesting copies of correspondence and communications from 20th October 1816, to 24th November, 1817, received at the Department of State from the American commissioner under the fourth article of the treaty of Ghent, I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, with the copies of papers mentioned in the resolution.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
