John Quincy Adams
January 20, 1826

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 23d ultimo, I transmit herewith reports* from the Secretary of War and the Commissioner of the General Land Office, with the statements desired by the resolution.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*Relating to land warrants issued to soldiers of the Revolutionary war, etc.
