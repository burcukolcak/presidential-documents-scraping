John Quincy Adams
May 05, 1828

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I communicate to the Senate, for their consideration and advice, a treaty of commerce and navigation between the United States and His Majesty the King of Prussia, signed on the 1st instant at this place by the Secretary of State and the charge' d'affaires of Prussia residing here. A copy of the treaty is also transmitted.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
