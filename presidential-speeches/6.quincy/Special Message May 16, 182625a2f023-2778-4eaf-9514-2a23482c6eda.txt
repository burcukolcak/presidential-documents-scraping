John Quincy Adams
May 16, 1826

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 9th instant, I communicate herewith a report* from the Secretary of the Treasury, with the documents desired by the resolution.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*Respecting the right of a foreign minister to retain money advanced by the President as an outfit beyond the sum appropriated by law.
