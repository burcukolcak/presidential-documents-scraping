John Quincy Adams
May 21, 1828

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I transmit to the House a report* from the Secretary of State, with a copy of the note of the minister of the United States to Spain dated 20th January, 1826, requested by a resolution of the House of the 19th instant.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*Relating to the war between Spain and her colonies.
