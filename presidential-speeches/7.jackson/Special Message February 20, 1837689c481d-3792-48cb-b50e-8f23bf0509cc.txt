Andrew Jackson
February 20, 1837

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 9th ultimo, I transmit a report from the Secretary of State and the documents * by which it was accompanied.
ANDREW JACKSON
* Correspondence of William Tudor, jr., while consul, etc., of the United States to Peru and charge d'affaires at Rio de Janeiro.
