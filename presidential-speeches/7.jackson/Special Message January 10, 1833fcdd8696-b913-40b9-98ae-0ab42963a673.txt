Andrew Jackson
January 10, 1833

Special Message
The SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES:
In compliance with the resolution of the House of the 4th instant, requesting to be furnished with such information as the President may possess "in relation to the survey of the northern boundary of the State of Ohio under the provisions of the act of Congress passed for that purpose on the 14th of July, 1832," I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of War containing it.
ANDREW JACKSON
