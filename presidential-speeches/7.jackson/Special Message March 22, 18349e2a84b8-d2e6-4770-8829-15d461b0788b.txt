Andrew Jackson
March 22, 1834

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I transmit to the House of Representatives a report+ from the Secretary of State, upon the subject of a resolution of the 10th instant, which was referred to that officer.
+Transmitting correspondence and papers relating to the claim of Don Juan Madrazp, a Spanish subject, for losses occasioned by acts of the United States and Georgia.
ANDREW JACKSON
