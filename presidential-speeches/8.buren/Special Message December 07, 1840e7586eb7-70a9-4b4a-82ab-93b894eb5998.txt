Martin van Buren
December 07, 1840

Special Message
Hon. R. M. T. HUNTER,
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
SIR: I herewith transmit a letter from the Secretary of the Navy, in relation to the navy pension fund, to which the attention of Congress is invited, and recommend an immediate appropriation of $151,352.39 to meet the payment of pensions becoming due on and after the 1st of January, 1841.
M. VAN BUREN.
