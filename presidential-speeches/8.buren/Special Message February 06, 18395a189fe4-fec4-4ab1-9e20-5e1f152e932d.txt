Martin van Buren
February 06, 1839

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 19th December last, I communicate to the Senate a report + from the Secretary of State, accompanying copies of the correspondence called for by said resolution.
M. VAN BUREN.
+ Relating to the commerce and navigation carried on within the Turkish dominions and in the Pashalic of Egypt.
