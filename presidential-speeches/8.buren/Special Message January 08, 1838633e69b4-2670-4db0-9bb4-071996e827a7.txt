Martin van Buren
January 08, 1838

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 5th instant, respecting the capture* and restoration of the Mexican brig of war the General Urrea, I transmit reports from the Secretaries of State and the Navy.
M. VAN BUREN.
* By the United States sloop of warNatchez off the coast of Texas.
