Martin van Buren
June 04, 1838

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I transmit herewith to the House of Representatives a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers, relating to the claim of the orphan children of Peter Shackerly,+ in answer to their resolution of the 28th ultimo.
M. VAN BUREN.
(+Killed on board of the United States shipChesapeakewhen attacked by the British ship of warLeopard, June 22, 1807.)
