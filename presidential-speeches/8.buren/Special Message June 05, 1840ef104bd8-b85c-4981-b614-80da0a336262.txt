Martin van Buren
June 05, 1840

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the Senate dated the 30th December, 1839, I transmit herewith the report* of the Secretary of War, furnishing so much of the information called-for by said resolution as relates to the Executive Department under his charge.
M. VAN BUREN.
*Relating to the refusal of banks to pay the Government demands in specie since the general resumption in 1838, and the payment of Government creditors in depreciated currency.
