Martin van Buren
March 31, 1840

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I communicate to the House of Representatives a report** from the Secretary of State, with documents, containing the information called for by their resolution of the 23d instant.
M. VAN BUREN.
**Relating to the demand of the minister of Spain for the surrender of the schoonerAmistad , with Africans on board, detained by the American brig of warWashington. etc.
