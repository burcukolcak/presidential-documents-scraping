Martin van Buren
October 02, 1837

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 13th ultimo, concerning the boundary between the United States and the Mexican Republic and a cession of territory belonging to the Mexican Confederation to the United States, I transmit a report from the Secretary of State and the documents by which it was accompanied.
M. VAN BUREN.
