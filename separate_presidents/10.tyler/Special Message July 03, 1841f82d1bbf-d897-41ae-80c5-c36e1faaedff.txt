John Tyler
July 03, 1841

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 9th instant (ultimo), I communicate to that body a report from the Secretary of State, conveying copies of the correspondence,* which contains all the information called for by said resolution.
JOHN TYLER.
*Relating to the duties levied on American tobacco imported Into the States composing the German Commercial and Custom-House Union.
