Millard Fillmore
July 10, 1850

Order of the President [on death of Zachary Taylor]
In consequence of the death of the President of the United States, I direct that the several Executive Departments be closed until after the funeral of the illustrious deceased, and that they, as well as the Executive Mansion, be placed in mourning, and that the several officers of the Government wear the usual badge of mourning for the term of six months.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
