Millard Fillmore
August 24, 1850

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I have the honor to transmit herewith a report submitted by the Secretary of the Treasury, to whom was referred the resolution of the Senate of the 31st July last, requesting to be furnished with certain information in relation to the commerce, etc., of the district of Brazos Santiago, in Texas.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
