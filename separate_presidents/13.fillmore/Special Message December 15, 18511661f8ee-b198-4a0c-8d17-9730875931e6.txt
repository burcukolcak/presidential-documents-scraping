Millard Fillmore
December 15, 1851

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit to the Senate a report * of the Secretary of State, in answer to their resolution of the 8th of March last.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
*Relating to the free navigation of the St. Lawrence, St. John, and other large rivers, and to the free enjoyment of the British North American fisheries by United States citizens.
