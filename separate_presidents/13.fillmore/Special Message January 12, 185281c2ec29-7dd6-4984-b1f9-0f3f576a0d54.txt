Millard Fillmore
January 12, 1852

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 5th instant, I herewith transmit to it a report and accompanying papers* from the Secretary of State.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
*Relating to a circular issued by the secretary of state for the British colonial department relative to the employment in the British West India colonies of free blacks and liberated slaves from the United States.
