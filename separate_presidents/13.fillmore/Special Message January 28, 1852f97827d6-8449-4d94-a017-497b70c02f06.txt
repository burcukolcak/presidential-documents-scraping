Millard Fillmore
January 28, 1852

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 15th ultimo, requesting information respecting the seizure and confiscation of the barkGeorgiana,of Maine, and brigSusan Loud,of Massachusetts,* I transmit a report from the Secretary of State and the documents which accompanied it.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
*By the Spanish or Cuban authorities.
