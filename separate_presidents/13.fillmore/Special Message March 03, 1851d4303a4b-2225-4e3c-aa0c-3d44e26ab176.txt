Millard Fillmore
March 03, 1851

Special Message
To the Senators of the United States, respectively.
SIR: Whereas divers and weighty causes connected with executive business necessary to be transacted create an extraordinary occasion requiring that the Senate be convened, you are therefore requested, as a member of that body, to attend a meeting thereof to be holden at the Capitol, in the city of Washington, on the 4th day of March instant.
MILLARD FILLMORE.
