Franklin Pierce
April 09, 1856

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying document,* in compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 4th instant.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
*Dispatch from the United States minister at Naples relative to the saving from shipwreck of certain American vessels and their crews by officers of the Neapolitan navy and marine service.
