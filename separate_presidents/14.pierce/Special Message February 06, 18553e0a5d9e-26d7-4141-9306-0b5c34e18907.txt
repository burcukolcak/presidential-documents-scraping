Franklin Pierce
February 06, 1855

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report + from the Secretary of State, in answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 27th ultimo.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
+ Stating that the information relative to the applicability to the Spanish colonies of the treaty of 1795 with Spain, and whether American citizens residing in said colonies are entitled to the benefits of its provisions, had been already transmitted
