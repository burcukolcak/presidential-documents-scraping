Franklin Pierce
June 18, 1856

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents,* in answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 16th instant.
FRANKLIN PIERCE.
*Instructions to Mr. Buchanan, late minister to England, on the subject of free ships making free goods, and letter from Mr. Buchanan to Lord Clarendon on the same subject.
