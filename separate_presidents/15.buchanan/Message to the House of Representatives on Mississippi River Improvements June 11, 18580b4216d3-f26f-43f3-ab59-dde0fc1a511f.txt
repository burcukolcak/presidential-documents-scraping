James Buchanan
June 11, 1858

Message to the House of Representatives on Mississippi River Improvements
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of War, with the accompanying papers,* in obedience to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 2d of June, 1858.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
*Copies of contracts for deepening the channels of the Southwest Pass and Pass a l'Outre, at the mouth of the Mississippi River, etc.
