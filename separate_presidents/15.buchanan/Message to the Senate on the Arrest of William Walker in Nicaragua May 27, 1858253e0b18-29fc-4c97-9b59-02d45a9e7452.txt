James Buchanan
May 27, 1858

Message to the Senate on the Arrest of William Walker in Nicaragua
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith, in compliance with the resolution of the Senate of the 19th of May, a communication from the Secretary of the Navy with copies of the correspondence, etc.,* as afforded by the files of the Department.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
*Relating to the arrest of William Walker and associates within the territory of Nicaragua by the naval forces under Commodore Paulding.
