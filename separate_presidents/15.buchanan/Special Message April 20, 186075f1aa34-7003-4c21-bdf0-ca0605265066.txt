James Buchanan
April 20, 1860

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report of the Secretary of the Navy, to whom was referred the resolution of the House of Representatives of April 1860, requesting the President to communicate to the House, in addition to the information asked in the resolution adopted in reference to the African slave trade, "the number of officers and men in the service of the United States belonging to the African Squadron who have died in that service since the date of the Ashburton treaty up to the present time."
JAMES BUCHANAN.
