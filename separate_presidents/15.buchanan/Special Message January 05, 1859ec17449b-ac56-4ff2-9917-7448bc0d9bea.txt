James Buchanan
January 05, 1859

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith, for the constitutional action of the Senate, the articles of agreement and convention made and concluded on the 19th day of June last (1858) with the Sisseeton and Wahpaton bands of the Dakota or Sioux Indians, with accompanying papers from the Department of the Interior.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
