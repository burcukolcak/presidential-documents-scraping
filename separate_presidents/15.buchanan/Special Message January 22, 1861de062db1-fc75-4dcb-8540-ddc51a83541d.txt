James Buchanan
January 22, 1861

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I herewith transmit to the House of Representatives a communication from the Secretary of the Navy, with accompanying reports, of the persons who were sent to the Isthmus of Chiriqui to make the examinations required by the fifth section of the act making appropriations for the naval service, approvedJune 22, 1860.
JAMES BUCHANAN.
