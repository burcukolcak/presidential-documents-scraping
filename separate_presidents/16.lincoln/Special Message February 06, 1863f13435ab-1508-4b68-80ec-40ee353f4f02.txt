Abraham Lincoln
February 06, 1863

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the Senate of the United States of yesterday, requesting information in regard to the death of General Ward, a citizen of the United States in the military service of the Chinese Government, I transmit a copy of a dispatch of the 27th of October last, and of its accompaniment, from the minister of the United States in China.
ABRAHAM LINCOLN.
