Abraham Lincoln
January 31, 1862

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
As a sequel to the correspondence on the subject previously communicated, I transmit to Congress extracts from a dispatch of the 20th ultimo from Mr. Adams, United States minister at London, to the Secretary of State, and a copy of an instruction from Earl Russell to Lord Lyons of the 10th instant, relative to the removal of certain citizens of the United States from the British mail steamerTrentby order of the commander of the United States war steamerSan Jacinto.
ABRAHAM LINCOLN.
