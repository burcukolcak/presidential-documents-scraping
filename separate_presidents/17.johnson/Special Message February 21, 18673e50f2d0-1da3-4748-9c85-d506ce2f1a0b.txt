Andrew Johnson
February 21, 1867

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit to the House of Representatives, in answer to their resolution of the 14th instant, a report* from the Secretary of State of this date.
ANDREW JOHNSON.
*Stating that the correspondence relative to the refusal of the United States consul at Cadiz, Spain, to certify invoices of wines shipped from that port had been sent to the Senate.
