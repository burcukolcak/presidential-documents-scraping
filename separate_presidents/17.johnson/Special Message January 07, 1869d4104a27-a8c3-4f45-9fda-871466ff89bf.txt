Andrew Johnson
January 07, 1869

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith, in answer to a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 16th of December last, a report* from the Secretary of State of the 6th instant.
ANDREW JOHNSON.
* Giving reasons why reductions in the number of officers and employees and in the salaries and expenses of the Department of State should not be made.
