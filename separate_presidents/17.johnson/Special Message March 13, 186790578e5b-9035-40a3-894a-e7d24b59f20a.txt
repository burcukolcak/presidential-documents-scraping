Andrew Johnson
March 13, 1867

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I herewith lay before the Senate, for its constitutional action thereon, treaty concluded on the 27th instant (ultimo) between the United States and the Pottawatomie tribe of Indians.
A letter of the Secretary of the Interior of the 28th instant (ultimo) and a copy of a communication from the Commissioner of Indian Affairs on the 27th instant (ultimo), explanatory of the said treaty, are also herewith transmitted.
ANDREW JOHNSON.
