Ulysses S. Grant
December 03, 1872

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith to Congress a report, dated the 2d instant, with the accompanying papers,* received from the Secretary of State, in compliance with the requirements of the eighteenth section of the act entitled "An act to regulate the diplomatic and consular systems of the United States," approved August 18, 1856.
U.S. GRANT
*Report of fees collected, etc., by consular officers of the United States for 1871, and tariff of consular fees.
