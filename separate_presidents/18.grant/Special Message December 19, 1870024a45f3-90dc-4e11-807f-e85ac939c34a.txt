Ulysses S. Grant
December 19, 1870

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit to the House of Representatives a report of the Secretary of State and the papers* by which it was accompanied, in answer to its resolution of the 7th instant.
U. S. GRANT.
*Relating to the seizure at Port Hood, Nova Scotia, by a Canadian revenue cutter, of the schooner "Granada", of Provincetown, Mass.
