Ulysses S. Grant
February 06, 1874

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit to the House of Representatives, in answer to their resolution of the 16th ultimo, a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers.*
U.S. GRANT
*Correspondence relative to the refusal of the United States consul at Cadiz, Spain, to certify invoices of wine shipped from that port, etc.
