Ulysses S. Grant
February 15, 1877

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith, in answer to the resolution of the Senate of the 13th instant, a report from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers.*
U.S. GRANT.
*Statements of appropriations and expenditures of the Department of State from March 4, 1789, to June 30, 1876, inclusive.
