Ulysses S. Grant
January 08, 1877

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
In answer to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 19th ultimo, l transmit herewith the report of the Secretary of State, together with the papers* which accompanied it.
U.S. GRANT.
*Correspondence relative to the Venezuelan mixed commission held under the convention of April 25, 1866, for the settlement of claims against Venezuela.
