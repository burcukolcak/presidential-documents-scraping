Ulysses S. Grant
May 19, 1876

Special Message
To the Senate:
I transmit herewith, in answer to a resolution of the Senate of the 27th March last, a report* from the Secretary of State and an accompanying paper.
U.S. GRANT.
*Relating to amount of money in the custody of the Department of State to the credit of the awards of the mixed commission under the treaty with Venezuela of April 25, 1866.
