Chester A. Arthur
October 12, 1881

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit herewith to the Senate a communication from the Secretary of State, submitting the text, in the English and French languages, of the proceedings of the International Sanitary Conference, provided for by the joint resolution of the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America, held at Washington in the early part of 1881.
CHESTER A. ARTHUR
