Grover Cleveland
April 02, 1888

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, with its inclosures, in response to the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 8th ultimo, in relation to affairs in Samoa.
GROVER CLEVELAND
(A similar message was sent to the Senate in answer to a resolution of that body of December 21, 1887.)
