Grover Cleveland
January 23, 1888

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith the first report of the board of control created by the act of Congress approved August 4, 1886 (24 U. S. Statutes at Large, p. 252), for the management of an industrial home in the Territory of Utah, containing a statement of the action of the board in establishing the home and account of expenditures from the appropriation made for that purpose in the act above mentioned.
GROVER CLEVELAND
