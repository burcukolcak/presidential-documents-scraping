Grover Cleveland
January 22, 1896

Special Message
To the House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith, in compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of December 28, 1895, a report from the Secretary of State, with copies of all the correspondence of record in the Department of State in relation to the schoonerHenry Crosby,fired upon while at anchor at Azua, Santo Domingo, December 10, 1893.
GROVER CLEVELAND.
