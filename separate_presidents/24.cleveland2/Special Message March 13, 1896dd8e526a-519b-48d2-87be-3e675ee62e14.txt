Grover Cleveland
March 13, 1896

Special Message
To the Senate.
I transmit herewith, in response to a resolution of the Senate of March 2, a report from the Secretary of State, accompanied by copies of correspondence touching the arrest in Havana of Marcus E. Rodriguez, Luis Someillauy Azpeitia, and Luis Someillauy Vidal, citizens of the United States.
GROVER CLEVELAND.
