Theodore Roosevelt
December 15, 1903

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, with companying papers, concerning the claim of Lieut. Col. L. K. Scott, a British subject, on account of the adoption by the Ordnance Department of the United States Army of a system of sighting of which he is the inventor.
THEODORE ROOSEVELT.
