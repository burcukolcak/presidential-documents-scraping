Theodore Roosevelt
January 23, 1905

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
I transmit herewith a communication from the Secretary of State covering the report of the agent of the United States in the arbitration of the Venezuelan cases before The Hague tribunal, with accompanying appendixes.
The attention of Congress is invited to the request of the Secretary of State that 500 copies of the report and appendixes be printed for the use of the Department of State.
THEODORE ROOSEVELT.
