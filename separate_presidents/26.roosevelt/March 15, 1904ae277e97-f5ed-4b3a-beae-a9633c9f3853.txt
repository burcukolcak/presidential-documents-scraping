Theodore Roosevelt
March 15, 1904

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives:
transmit herewith, for the information of Congress, a letter from the Secretary of State submitting a copy of the report of the commissioner appointed to carry out the resolution with respect to the Pan-American Railway, adopted by the second international conference of American States, held in the City of Mexico during the winter of 1901-2.
THEODORE ROOSEVELT.
