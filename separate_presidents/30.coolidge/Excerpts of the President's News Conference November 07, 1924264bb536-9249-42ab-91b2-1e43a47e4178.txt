Calvin Coolidge
November 07, 1924

Excerpts of the President's News Conference
I haven't any legislative program that I can announce at the present time. Naturally that brings up the old question of whether I ought to deliver my message to the Congress to the newspapermen before I deliver it to the Congress. I think that you usually get the best of that.
Source: "The Talkative President: The Off-the-Record Press Conferences of Calvin Coolidge". eds. Howard H. Quint & Robert H. Ferrell. The University Massachusetts Press. 1964.
