Herbert Hoover
June 28, 1930

Letter Commending Russell Owen on His Reporting of the Byrd Antarctic Expedition.
[Released June 28, 1930. Dated June 27, 1930]
My dear Mr. Owen:
I congratulate you most cordially upon your remarkably interesting and human news reports of the Byrd Antarctic expedition. They were splendid.

Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. Russell Owen, New York, N.Y.]
Note: The message was read at a dinner in New York honoring Mr. Owen, a correspondent for the New York Times. Mr. Owen spent nearly 2 years in Antarctica with Rear Adm. Richard E. Byrd and won the Pulitzer Prize for the most outstanding reportorial work of 1929.
