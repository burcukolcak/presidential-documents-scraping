Herbert Hoover
August 04, 1931

Message Congratulating Colonel and Mrs. Edward M. House on Their Golden Wedding Anniversary.
MRS. HOOVER and I should like to join with your legion of friends in extending heartiest congratulations to you and Mrs. House on your golden wedding anniversary. We wish for you a plentitude of health and happiness.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Colonel Edward M. House, Manchester, Mass. ]
