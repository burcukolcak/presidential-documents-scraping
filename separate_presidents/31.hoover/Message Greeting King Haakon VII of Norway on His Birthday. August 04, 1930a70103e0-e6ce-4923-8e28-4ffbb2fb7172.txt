Herbert Hoover
August 04, 1930

Message Greeting King Haakon VII of Norway on His Birthday.
[Released August 4, 1930. Dated August 3, 1930]
ON BEHALF of the Government and people of the United States in my own name, I offer Your Majesty hearty congratulations on this auspicious anniversary.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Majesty Haakon VII, King of Norway, Oslo]
