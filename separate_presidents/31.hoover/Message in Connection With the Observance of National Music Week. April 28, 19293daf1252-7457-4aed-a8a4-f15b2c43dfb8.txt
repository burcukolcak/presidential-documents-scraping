Herbert Hoover
April 28, 1929

Message in Connection With the Observance of National Music Week.
[Released April 28, 1929. Dated April 16, 1929]
CULTURAL DEVELOPMENT of a people must proceed in step with material well-being, if they are to enjoy the fullest happiness. Musical appreciation should therefore be promoted, as one of the liberal arts of life.
HERBERT HOOVER
Note: The message was sent to C. M. Tremaine, secretary of the National Music Week Committee.
