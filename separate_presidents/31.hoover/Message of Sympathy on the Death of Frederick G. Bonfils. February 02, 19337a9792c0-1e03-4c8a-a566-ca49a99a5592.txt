Herbert Hoover
February 02, 1933

Message of Sympathy on the Death of Frederick G. Bonfils.
I AM GRIEVED to learn of the death of your husband. Mrs. Hoover joins with me in expressing our deep sympathy.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mrs. Frederick G. Bonfils, Denver, Colorado]
Note: Mr. Bonfils was publisher of the Denver Post and founder of the Sells-Floto Circus.
