Herbert Hoover
November 26, 1932

Message of Sympathy on the Death of Leonor Llorente de Calles.
MRS. HOOVER and I are very unhappy to learn of the death of Mrs. Calles and wish to express our sincere sympathy to you and your family in your great loss.
HERBERT HOOVER
[General Plutarco Elias Calles, Mexico City, Mexico]
Note: Mrs. Calles, wife of the former President of Mexico, died at her home in Mexico City on November 25, 1932.
