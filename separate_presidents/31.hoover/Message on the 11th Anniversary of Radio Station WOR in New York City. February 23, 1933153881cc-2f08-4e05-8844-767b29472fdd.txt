Herbert Hoover
February 23, 1933

Message on the 11th Anniversary of Radio Station WOR in New York City.
I HEARTILY congratulate the management of Station WOR upon the celebration of the eleventh anniversary of its noteworthy service in the field of radio broadcasting, so valuable in supplying the people with news, entertainment and facts useful in the formation of public opinion.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. Alfred J. McCosker, 1440 Broadway, New York, N.Y.]
Note: The message was made public by Mr. McCosker, director and general manager of the station.
