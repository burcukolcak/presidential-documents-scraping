Herbert Hoover
April 08, 1932

Message on the Celebration of Parents' Day.
[Released April 8, 1932. Dated April 5, 1932]
THE FINEST partnership in the world is the partnership of father, mother, and children, in the business of making one another happy.

[Uncle Robert, Uncle Robert Foundation, 18 East 15th Street, New York City]
Note: The message was issued in conjunction with publicity for the Parents' Day celebration on May 8, 1932, on the Central Park Mall in New York City.
Uncle Robert was a popular children's radio entertainer and sponsor of a number of civic and charitable activities.
