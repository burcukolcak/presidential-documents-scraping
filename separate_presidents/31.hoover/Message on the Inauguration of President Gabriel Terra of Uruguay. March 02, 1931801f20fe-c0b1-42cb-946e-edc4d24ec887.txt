Herbert Hoover
March 02, 1931

Message on the Inauguration of President Gabriel Terra of Uruguay.
[Released March 2, 1931. Dated March 1, 1931]
ON THIS DAY of good auspices in the destiny of Uruguay, permit me to offer Your Excellency, with my most cordial congratulations, my heartiest good wishes for your happiness in the high office to which you have been led by the confidence and esteem of your fellow countrymen.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Gabriel Terra, President of The Oriental Republic of Uruguay, Montevideo, Uruguay ]
