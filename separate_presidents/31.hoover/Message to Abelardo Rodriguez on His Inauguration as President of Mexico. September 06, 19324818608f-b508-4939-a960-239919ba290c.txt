Herbert Hoover
September 06, 1932

Message to Abelardo Rodriguez on His Inauguration as President of Mexico.
UPON THE OCCASION of Your Excellency's inauguration as Substitute Constitutional President of Mexico, I wish to express my sincere good wishes for the success of your administration and for the prosperity and happiness of the people of Mexico.
HERBERT HOOVER
Note: On September 4, 1932, the Mexican Congress unanimously elected General Rodriguez to succeed Pascual Ortiz Rubio who had resigned as President of Mexico on September 3.
