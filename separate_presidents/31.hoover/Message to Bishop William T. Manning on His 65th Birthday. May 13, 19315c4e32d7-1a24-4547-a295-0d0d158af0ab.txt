Herbert Hoover
May 13, 1931

Message to Bishop William T. Manning on His 65th Birthday.
I MOST CORDIALLY congratulate you upon the triple celebration of. this day in your life when your sixty-fifth birthday coincides with the fortieth anniversary of your ministry and the tenth of your bishopric and I send you best wishes for every blessing in the years to come.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Right Reverend William T. Manning, Bishop's House, Cathedral Heights, New York City ]
Note: William T. Manning was Bishop of the New York Episcopate of the Protestant Episcopal Church. The message was made public in conjunction with a festival service held in the Cathedral of St. John the Divine.
