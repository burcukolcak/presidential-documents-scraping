Herbert Hoover
March 08, 1931

Message to Justice Oliver Wendell Holmes On His 90th Birthday.
[Released March 8, 1931. Dated March 7, 1931]
My dear Mr. Justice:
I most cordially congratulate you upon your ninetieth birthday anniversary, but yet more do I congratulate our country upon the continuance of your splendid services, and hope that you may long live in health and strength to carry them forward.

Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Hon. Oliver Wendell Holmes, 1720 Eye Street, N.W., Washington, D.C.]
Note: The message was made public in conjunction with ceremonies in Washington, D.C., honoring Associate Justice Holmes. The ceremonies were broadcast over the Columbia Broadcasting System radio network.
