Herbert Hoover
June 03, 1930

Message to King George V of Great Britain Offering Birthday Greetings.
ON BEHALF of my fellow countrymen and in my own name, I take especial pleasure in extending to Your Majesty hearty greetings of friendship and good will on this birthday. May Your Majesty be blest with health and happiness for many years to come.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Majesty King George V, London, England]
