Herbert Hoover
May 10, 1929

Message to King Michael of Rumania on Rumania's Independence Day.
ON THIS happy occasion of the anniversary of Rumanian independence, I send to Your Majesty sincere felicitations and cordial wishes for Your Majesty's welfare and the continued prosperity of the Rumanian nation.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Majesty Michael I, King of Rumania, Care of President of the Regency Council, Bucharest]
