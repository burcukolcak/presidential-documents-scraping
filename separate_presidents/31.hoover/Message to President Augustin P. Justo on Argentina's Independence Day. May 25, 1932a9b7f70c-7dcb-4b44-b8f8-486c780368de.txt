Herbert Hoover
May 25, 1932

Message to President Augustin P. Justo on Argentina's Independence Day.
ON THIS national holiday when Argentina celebrates the anniversary of its independence I take pleasure in extending to Your Excellency sincere congratulations and earnest wishes of my Government in which I join most heartily for the continued prosperity of your Nation.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Augustin P. Justo, President of Argentina, Buenos Aires, Argentina]
