Herbert Hoover
December 11, 1929

Message to President Augusto B. Leguia on the Inauguration of Direct Radio Service Between Peru and the United States.
[Released December 11, 1929. Dated December 10, 1929]
UPON THE OCCASION of the inauguration of the All American Cables station at Lima, I wish to extend to you and to the people of Peru, on behalf of myself and the people of the United States, every good wish for the prosperity and continued well-being of the people of Peru.
