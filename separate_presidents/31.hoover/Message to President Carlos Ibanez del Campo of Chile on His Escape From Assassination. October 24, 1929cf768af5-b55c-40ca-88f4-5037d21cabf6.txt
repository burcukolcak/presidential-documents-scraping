Herbert Hoover
October 24, 1929

Message to President Carlos Ibanez del Campo of Chile on His Escape From Assassination.
I AM most gratified to learn of your fortunate escape from the attempt on your life yesterday.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Carlos Ibanez del Campo, President of Chile, Santiago]
Note: The assassination attempt by a young man identified as an anarchist failed when his firearm malfunctioned.
