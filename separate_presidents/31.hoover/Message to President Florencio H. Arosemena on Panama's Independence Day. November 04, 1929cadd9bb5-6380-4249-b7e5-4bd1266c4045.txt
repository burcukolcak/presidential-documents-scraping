Herbert Hoover
November 04, 1929

Message to President Florencio H. Arosemena on Panama's Independence Day.
[Released November 4, 1929. Dated November 3, 1929]
ON BEHALF of my fellow-countrymen and in my own name I send to Your Excellency cordial felicitations on the occasion of this auspicious anniversary.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Florencio H. Arosemena, The President of Panama, Panama City]
