Herbert Hoover
January 02, 1930

Message to President Louis Borno on Haiti's Independence Day.
[Released January 2, 1930. Dated January 1, 1930]
I HAVE pleasure in extending to Your Excellency and to the people of Haiti cordial greetings on this anniversary of the independence of the Republic.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Louis Borno, The President of Haiti, Port au Prince]
