Herbert Hoover
October 02, 1932

Message to President Paul von Hindenburg of Germany on His Birthday.
ON THIS eighty-fifth anniversary of Your Excellency's birth, I send you my cordial felicitations. May your future have in store for you a long continuation of good health and happiness.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Field Marshal Paul von Beneckendorff und von Hindenburg, President of the Reich, Berlin, Germany]
