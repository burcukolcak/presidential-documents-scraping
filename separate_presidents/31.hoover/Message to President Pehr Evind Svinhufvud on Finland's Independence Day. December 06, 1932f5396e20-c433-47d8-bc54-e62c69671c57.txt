Herbert Hoover
December 06, 1932

Message to President Pehr Evind Svinhufvud on Finland's Independence Day.
ON THIS national anniversary which the people of Finland are celebrating today, I send to Your Excellency cordial greetings and the assurances of my high regard and best wishes.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Pehr Evind Svinhufvud, The President of Finland, Helsingfors, Finland ]
