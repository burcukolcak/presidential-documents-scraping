Herbert Hoover
December 04, 1930

Message to the Federation for the Support of Jewish Philanthropic Societies.
THE SPLENDID work of the Federation for the Support of the Jewish Philanthropic Societies deserves the most generous public support.
HERBERT HOOVER
Note: The message, sent to Solomon Lowenstein, executive director, Federation for the Support of Jewish Philanthropic Societies of New York City, 71 West 47th St., New York City, was read at a rally of philanthropic society workers and fundraisers held at the Hotel Biltmore in New York City.
