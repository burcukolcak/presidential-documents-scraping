Herbert Hoover
June 30, 1930

Message to the Kiwanis International Convention.
PLEASE EXTEND my cordial greetings to the Kiwanis International Convention and my hope that the Kiwanis movement everywhere may continue to advance those high ideals of public helpfulness and service which means so much to the progress of our civic life.
HERBERT HOOVER
Note: The message, sent to Horace W. McDavid, president, Kiwanis Interna-tional, Haddon Hall, Atlantic City, N.J., was read to the annual convention in Atlantic City, N.J.
