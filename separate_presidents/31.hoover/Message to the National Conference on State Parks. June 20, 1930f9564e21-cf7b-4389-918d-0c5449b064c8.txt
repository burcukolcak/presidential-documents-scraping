Herbert Hoover
June 20, 1930

Message to the National Conference on State Parks.
[Released June 20, 1930. Dated June 16, 1930]
PLEASE present my cordial greetings to the National Conference on State Parks and my deep appreciation of their service in the encouragement of the effort that States and individuals are making to preserve their scenic endowment and to provide the public with permanent facilities for access to natural beauty and outdoor recreation.
HERBERT HOOVER
Note: The message, sent to Herbert Evison, executive secretary, National Conference on State Parks, Linville, N.C., was read at a meeting off the Conference.
