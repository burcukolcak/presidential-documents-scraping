Herbert Hoover
October 12, 1929

Message to the President of Peru on His Reinauguration.
ON THIS auspicious occasion of Your Excellency's reinauguration to your high office I extend my hearty greetings and my best wishes for the continued success of your administration as well as for your own health and happiness.
HERBERT HOOVER
[His Excellency Augusto B. Legufa, The President of Peru, Lima]
