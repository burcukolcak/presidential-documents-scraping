Herbert Hoover
February 07, 1931

Message to the Real Estate Board of New York.
I WILL BE obliged if you will express my cordial greetings to those gathered at the thirty-fifth annual banquet of the Real Estate Board of New York and my best wishes for an inspiring meeting.
HERBERT HOOVER
[Peter Grimm, President, The Real Estate Board of New York, Inc., Hotel Commodore, New York City]
Note: The message was read at the annual dinner of the Real Estate Board of New York, held in the Commodore Hotel in New York City.
