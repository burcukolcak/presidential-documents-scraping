Herbert Hoover
September 25, 1931

Message to the Regional Conference of the New Jersey Chapters of the American National Red Cross.
[Released September 25, 1931. Dated September 22, 1931 ]
My dear Mr. Eisner:
Please present my greetings to those assembled for the annual Regional Conference of the American Red Cross at Fort Monmouth. I trust that the deliberations of the representatives of the New Jersey Chapters may be fruitful in effective plans to meet the current demands upon the organization.

Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. Monroe Eisner, 107B Monmouth Street, Red Bank, New Jersey]
Note: The message was read at the annual regional conference.
