Herbert Hoover
December 17, 1929

Message to the Tanners' Council.
My dear Mr. Moffatt:
Please express to the members of the Tanners' Council gathered in Washington my cordial greetings and my deep appreciation of their patriotic cooperation in the nationwide task of assuring stability to the economic structure of the country in this hour of stress.
Yours faithfully,
HERBERT HOOVER
[Mr. Fraser M. Moffatt, Tanners' Council, Washington, D.C.]
Note: Executives of the leather tanning industry were meeting to discuss actions to follow up suggestions made during the Chamber of Commerce Conference on December 5 (see Item 297).
