Herbert Hoover
July 23, 1931

Messages Congratulating the Secretary of State and the Secretary of the Treasury on Their Roles in the London Conference of Ministers.
SECRETARY OF STATE HENRY L. STIMSON
I WISH to convey to you my sincere congratulations. My appreciation is the deeper from knowing the complexity of the difficulties which confronted you over the past ten days and the able manner they have been surmounted. I hope you may now secure a real vacation.
HERBERT HOOVER
SECRETARY OF THE TREASURY ANDREW W. MELLON
I WISH again to congratulate you upon a most notable public service. I trust you may now secure a real vacation.
HERBERT HOOVER
