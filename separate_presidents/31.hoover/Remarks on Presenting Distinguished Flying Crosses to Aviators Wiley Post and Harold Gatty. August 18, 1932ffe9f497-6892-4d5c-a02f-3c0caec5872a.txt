Herbert Hoover
August 18, 1932

Remarks on Presenting Distinguished Flying Crosses to Aviators Wiley Post and Harold Gatty.
I COMMEND your flight around the world in 9 days. It was a monument to your courage and skill, and I want to express my admiration to you and the admiration of the world.
Note: The President spoke at presentation ceremonies at the White House.
