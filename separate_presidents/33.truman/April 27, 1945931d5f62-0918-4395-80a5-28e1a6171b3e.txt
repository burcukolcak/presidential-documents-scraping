Harry S. Truman
April 27, 1945

Statement by the President Announcing the Appointment of Dr. Isador Lubin to the Reparations Commission.
I WISH, also, to announce that I have asked Dr. Isador Lubin, Commissioner of Labor Statistics, to serve with and accompany Mr. Pauley as his associate.
Dr. Lubin will be given the personal rank of Minister. I am very grateful to Dr. Lubin for accepting this post.
