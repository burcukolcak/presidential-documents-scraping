Harry S. Truman
July 21, 1947

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Midyear Economic Report
THE WHITE HOUSE,

Washington, D.C, July 21, 1947.
The Honorable the PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE,

The Honorable the SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES.
SIRS: I am presenting herewith a Midyear Economic Report to the Congress. This is supplementary to the Economic Report of the President of January 8, 1947, and is transmitted in accordance with section 3 (b) of the Employment Act of 1946.
In preparing this report I have had the advice and assistance of the Council of Economic Advisers, members of the Cabinet, and heads of independent agencies.
Respectfully,


HARRY S. TRUMAN
