Dwight D. Eisenhower
April 16, 1955

Telegram to Senator Thurmond Saluting James F. Byrnes as a Great American.
The Honorable Strom Thurmond

c/o Aiken Chamber of Commerce

Aiken, South Carolina
Please give my warm greetings to your guest of honor, Jimmy Byrnes. I am fortunate to count him my good friend and with countless others salute him as a great American.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
Note: This telegram was sent in connection with a testimonial dinner given Mr. Byrnes by the Aiken Chamber of Commerce. It was released at Augusta, Ca.
