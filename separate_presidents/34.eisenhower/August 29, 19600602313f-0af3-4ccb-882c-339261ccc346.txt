Dwight D. Eisenhower
August 29, 1960

Message to President Po Sun Yun of Korea.
Dear Mr. President:
It is with great pleasure that I extend my congratulations and best wishes to you upon your assumption of the office of President of the Republic of Korea.
I wish you all success in discharging the responsibilities of this high office, your accession to which bears eloquent testimony to the trust and confidence reposed in you as a distinguished leader of a great people.

Sincerely,
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
