Dwight D. Eisenhower
December 15, 1956

Telegram to the President of Finland on the Death of Former President Paasikivi.
His Excellency

Urho K. Kekkonen

President of Finland
I have learned with regret of the passing of Finland's illustrious former President [Juho Kusti] Paasikivi. On behalf of the American people and myself I send sincere condolences to both you and the people of Finland in the loss of President Paasikivi who served his people so well through a difficult period in the history of Finland.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
