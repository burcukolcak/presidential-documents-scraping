Dwight D. Eisenhower
January 13, 1955

Cablegram to Dr. Albert Schweitzer on the Occasion of His 80th Birthday
Dr. Albert Schweitzer
Lambarene, French Equatorial Africa
My cordial greetings and best wishes on your eightieth birthday. Your spirit and work have been an example and inspiration to all of us.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
