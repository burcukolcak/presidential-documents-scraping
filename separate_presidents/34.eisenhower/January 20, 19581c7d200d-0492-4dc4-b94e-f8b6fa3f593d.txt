Dwight D. Eisenhower
January 20, 1958

Letter to Nikolai Bulganin, Chairman, Council of Ministers, U.S.S.R.
Dear Mr. Chairman:
I have received your letter of January eighth. I believe that I have dealt with the substantive matters which it contains in my message of January twelfth. Should further study of your letter indicate that additional response is appropriate, I shall communicate with you at a later date.

Sincerely,
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
Note: The text of Mr. Bulganin's letter of January 8, 1958, was not included in the release. See Item 31, below.
The President's message of January 12 appears as Item 7 above.
