Dwight D. Eisenhower
July 08, 1957

Statement by the President on the Death of Mrs. Grace Goodhue Coolidge.
I HAVE just been informed of the passing of Mrs. Grace Goodhue Coolidge, widow of the thirtieth President of the United States. Mrs. Eisenhower and I join with her many friends and admirers in expressing our deep regrets and our sympathies to her family.
