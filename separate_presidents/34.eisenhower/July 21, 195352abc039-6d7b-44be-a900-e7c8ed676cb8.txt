Dwight D. Eisenhower
July 21, 1953

Telegram Congratulating Ben Hogan.
Ben Hogan
- City Hall
New York, N.Y.
Millions of Americans would like to participate with the New Yorkers today who are extending their traditional welcome upon your return from your magnificent victory. We are proud of you not only as a great competitor and a master of your craft, but also as an envoy extraordinary in the business of building friendship for America. With best wishes to you and Mrs. Hogan.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
Note: The President's message was sent on the occasion of Ben Hogan's return to the United States after winning the British Open Golf Championship at Carnoustie, Scotland.
