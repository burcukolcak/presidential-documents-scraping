Dwight D. Eisenhower
March 20, 1956

Cablegram to the President of Lebanon Expressing Sympathy Following an Earthquake Disaster.
[Released March 20, 1956. Dated March 19, 1956]
His Excellency

Camille Chamoun

President of the Republic of Lebanon
News of the tragic consequences of the earthquakes in Lebanon has been received with sorrow and concern throughout the United States. On behalf of the American people I send deepest sympathy to Your Excellency and to all those who are suffering in this disaster.
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
