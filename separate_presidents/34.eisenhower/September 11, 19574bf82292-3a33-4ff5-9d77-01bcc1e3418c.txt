Dwight D. Eisenhower
September 11, 1957

Message to Chancellor Raab of Austria Regarding His Illness.
[Released September 11, 1957. Dated September 6, 1957]
Dear Chancellor Raab:
The news of your sudden illness was very distressing. I recall with pleasure the time we spent together while you were in the United States. I look forward to heating of your quick and complete recovery.

Sincerely,
DWIGHT D. EISENHOWER
Note: This message to His Excellency Julius Raab was released at the U. S. Naval Base, Newport, R. I.
