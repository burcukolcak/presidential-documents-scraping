John F. Kennedy
November 07, 1961

Telegram to Chairman Brezhnev on the Anniversary of the November Revolution in Russia.
[Released November 7, 1961- Dated November 6, 1961]
Leonid Brezhnev,

Chairman, Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, U.S.S.R.
On the occasion of this national holiday of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, the people of the United States join me in sending to the peoples of the Soviet Union our best wishes for a prosperous future in a secure and peaceful world.
JOHN F. KENNEDY
