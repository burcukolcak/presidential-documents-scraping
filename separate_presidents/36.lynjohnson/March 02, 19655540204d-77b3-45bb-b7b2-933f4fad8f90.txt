Lyndon B. Johnson
March 02, 1965

Special Message to the Congress on the Nation's Cities.
To the Congress of the United States:
Throughout man's history, the city* has been at the center of civilization. It is at the center of our own society.
* In this message the word city is used to mean the entire urban area--the central city and its suburbs. [Ed.
Note: For remarks upon signing related legislation, see Items 415, 503.
