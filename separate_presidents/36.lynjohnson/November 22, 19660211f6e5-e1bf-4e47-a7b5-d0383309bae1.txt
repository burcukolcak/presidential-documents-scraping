Lyndon B. Johnson
November 22, 1966

Birthday Telegram to Former Vice President John Nance Garner.
MRS. JOHNSON and I send our best wishes and warm regards and wish you a happy birthday and good health and comfort.
LYNDON B. JOHNSON
[Honorable John Nance Garner, Uvulae, Texas]
Note: Mr. Garner was born on November 22, 1868. He served as Vice President from March 4, 1933, to January 20, 1941.
The text of the telegram was posted on the bulletin board in the White House Press Office at Austin, Texas. It was not made public in the form of a White House press release.
