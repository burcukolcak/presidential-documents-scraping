Richard Nixon
August 09, 1974

Letter Resigning the Office of President of the United States
Dear Mr. Secretary:
I hereby resign the Office of President of the United States.
Sincerely,
RICHARD NIXON
[The Honorable Henry A. Kissinger, The Secretary of State, Washington, D.C. 20520]
Note: The President's letter of resignation was delivered to Secretary Kissinger in his White House office at 11:35 a.m., August 9, by Assistant to the President Alexander M. Haig, Jr.
