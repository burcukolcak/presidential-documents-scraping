Gerald R. Ford
April 29, 1976

Exchange With Reporters on Departure From Waco, Texas.
REPORTER. Mr. President, I understand today that you said that it was time for America to forget about President Nixon. Are you going to continue that policy if you are reelected, too?
THE PRESIDENT. Well, Mr. Nixon is a private citizen and-
Q. If he goes to China again, would you want another report from him?
THE PRESIDENT. Oh, well, I don't anticipate that he will. REPORTER. Thank you, Mr. President.
Note: The exchange began at 6:05 p.m. at James Connally Field, located on the Texas State Technical Institute campus.
