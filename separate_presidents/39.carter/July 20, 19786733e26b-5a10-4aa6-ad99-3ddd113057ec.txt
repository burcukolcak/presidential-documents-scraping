Jimmy Carter
July 20, 1978

Budget Rescission Message to the Congress. 
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith propose rescission of $500 million in unneeded budget authority that was made available to the Department of Labor for advances to the unemployment trust fund and other funds.
The details of the proposed rescission are contained in the attached report.
JIMMY CARTER

The White House,

July 20, 1978.
Note: The attachment detailing the rescission is printed in the FEDERAL REGISTER of July 25, 1978.
