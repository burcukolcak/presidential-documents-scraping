Ronald Reagan
June 24, 1986

Message to the Congress Reporting Budget Deferrals 
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report four new deferrals of budget authority totaling $46,424,273.
The details of these deferrals are contained in the attached report.
RONALD REAGAN

The White House,

June 24, 1986.
Note: The attachment detailing the deferrals was printed in the "Federal Register" of July 8.
