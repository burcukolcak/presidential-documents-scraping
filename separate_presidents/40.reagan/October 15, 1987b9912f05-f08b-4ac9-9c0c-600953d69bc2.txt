Ronald Reagan
October 15, 1987

Designation of Lucille G. Murchison as a Member of the Board of Governors of the United Service Organizations, Incorporated 
The President today announced his intention to redesignate Lucille G. Murchison to be a member of the Board of Governors of the United Service Organizations, Inc., for a term of 3 years.
Mrs. Murchison is a private investor. She was born November 4, 1925, in Dallas, TX. She has four children and currently resides in Dallas, TX.
