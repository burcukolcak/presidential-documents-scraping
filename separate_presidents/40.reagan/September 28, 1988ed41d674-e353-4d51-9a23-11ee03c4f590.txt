Ronald Reagan
September 28, 1988

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Annual Report of the Rehabilitation Services Administration 
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with Section 13 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended, I am pleased to transmit the annual report of the Rehabilitation Services Administration. The report, prepared by the Department of Education, covers activities supported under the Act in Fiscal Year 1987.
RONALD REAGAN

The White House,

September 28, 1988.
