George Bush
January 19, 1993

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Report on the National Security Strategy
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
In accordance with section 603 of the Goldwater-Nichols Department of Defense Reorganization Act of 1986 (Public Law 99 - 433; 50 U.S.C. 404a), I hereby transmit the annual report on the National Security Strategy of the United States.
Sincerely,
George Bush
Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Dan Quayle, President of the Senate.
