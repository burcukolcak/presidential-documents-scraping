George Bush
March 02, 1992

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Annual Report on Hazardous Materials Transportation
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the requirements of section 109(e) of the Hazardous Materials Transportation Act (Public Law 96 - 633; 49 U.S.C. 1808(e)), I transmit herewith the Annual Report on Hazardous Materials Transportation for calendar year 1990.
George Bush
The White House,
March 2, 1992.
