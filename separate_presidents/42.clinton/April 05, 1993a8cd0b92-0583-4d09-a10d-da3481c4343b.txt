William J. Clinton
April 05, 1993

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Hazardous Materials Transportation
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the requirements of section 109(e) of the Hazardous Materials Transportation Act (Public Law 93-633); 49 U.S.C. 108(e)), I transmit herewith the Annual Report on Hazardous Materials Transportation for calendar year 1991.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON

The White House,

April 5, 1993.
