William J. Clinton
April 05, 2000

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Hazardous Materials Transportation
To the Congress of the United States:
I herewith transmit the Department of Transportation's Biennial Report on Hazardous Materials Transportation for Calendar Years 19961997. The report has been prepared in accordance with the Federal hazardous materials transportation law, 49 U.S.C. 5121(e).
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
