William J. Clinton
April 12, 1996

Letter to Congressional Leaders Reporting Proposed Budget Rescissions
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report 10 proposed rescissions of budgetary resources, totaling $400.4 million. These rescission proposals affect the Department of Defense.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Newt Gingrich, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate. The report detailing the proposed rescissions was published in theFederal Registeron April 23.
