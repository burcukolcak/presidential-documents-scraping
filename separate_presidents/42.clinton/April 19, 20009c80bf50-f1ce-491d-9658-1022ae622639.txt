William J. Clinton
April 19, 2000

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Prevention of Nuclear Proliferation
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
As required under section 601(a) of the Nuclear Non-Proliferation Act of 1978, as amended (Public Law 95-242, 22 U.S.C. 3281(a)), I am transmitting a report on the United States Government's efforts to prevent nuclear proliferation. The report covers activities between January 1, 1998, and December 31, 1998.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
