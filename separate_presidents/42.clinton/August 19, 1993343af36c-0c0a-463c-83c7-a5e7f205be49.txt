William J. Clinton
August 19, 1993

Memorandum on Determination Pursuant to Section 2(b)(2) of the Mirgration and Refugee Assistance Act of 1962, as Amended
Presidential Determination No. 93-33
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Pursuant to section 2(b)(2) of the Migration and Refugee Assistance Act of 1962, as amended, 22 U.S.C. 2601(b)(2), I hereby designate African refugees who have returned to their countries of origin as qualifying for reintegration assistance, and determine that such assistance will contribute to the foreign policy interests of the United States.
You are authorized and directed to inform the appropriate committees of the Congress of this determination and the obligation of funds under this authority, and to publish this determination in theFederal Register.

WILLIAM J. CLINTON
THE WHITE HOUSE,
Washington, August 19, 1993.
