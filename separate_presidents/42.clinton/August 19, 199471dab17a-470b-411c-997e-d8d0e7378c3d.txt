William J. Clinton
August 19, 1994

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on United States Activities in the United Nations
To The Congress of the United States:
I am pleased to transmit herewith a report of the activities of the United States Government in the United Nations and its affiliated agencies during the calendar year 1993. The report is required by the United Nations Participation Act (Public Law 264, 79th Congress; 22 U.S.C. 287b).
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, August 19, 1994.
