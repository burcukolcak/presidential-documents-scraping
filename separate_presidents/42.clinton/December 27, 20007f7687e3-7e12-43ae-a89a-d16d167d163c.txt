William J. Clinton
December 27, 2000

Memorandum on Funding for International Financial Institutions and Other International Organizations and Programs
Presidential Determination No. 2001-08
Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject:Determination Pursuant to Section 523 of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 2001 (Public Law 106-429)
Pursuant to section 523 of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 2001 (Public Law 106-429), I hereby certify that withholding from international financial institutions and other international organizations and programs funds appropriated or otherwise made available pursuant to that Act is contrary to the national interest.
You are authorized and directed to publish this determination in the Federal Register
William J. Clinton
Note: This memorandum was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on December 28. An original was not available for verification of the content of this memorandum.
APP NOTE: This determination was incorrectly numbered "2000-08" in the Weekly Compilation of Presidential Documents, but was correctly numbered "2001-08" when published in the Federal Register.
