William J. Clinton
December 30, 1995

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Loan Guarantees to Israel
To the Congress of the United States:
Enclosed is an unclassified report on the Loan Guarantees to Israel Program and on economic conditions in Israel, as required by section 226(k) of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended (Public Law 87-195), and section 1205 of the International Security and Development Cooperation Act of 1985 (Public Law 99-983).
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, December 30, 1995.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on January 2, 1996.
