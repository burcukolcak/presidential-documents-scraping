William J. Clinton
February 02, 1994

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Railroad Safety
To the Congress of the United States:
I transmit herewith the 1992 annual report on the Administration of the Federal Railroad Safety Act of 1970, pursuant to section 211 of the Act (45 U.S.C. 440(a)).
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, February 2, 1994.
