William J. Clinton
February 12, 1993

Exchange With Reporters Prior to a Meeting With Congressional Leaders
Q. Mr. President, what kind of feedback have you been getting, sir? What kind of feedback you've been getting from the Congressmen andâ€”

Q. Do you think the Republicans will go along with you?
The President. Well, I don't know. Right now, I'm gathering their ideas and opinions, and we'll see. I hope so.

The President. Tune in Wednesday.
