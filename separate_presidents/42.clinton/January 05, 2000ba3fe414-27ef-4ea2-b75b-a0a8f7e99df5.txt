William J. Clinton
January 05, 2000

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on the National Security Strategy
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
As required by section 603 of the Goldwater-Nichols Department of Defense Reorganization Act of 1986, I am transmitting a report on the National Security Strategy of the United States.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
