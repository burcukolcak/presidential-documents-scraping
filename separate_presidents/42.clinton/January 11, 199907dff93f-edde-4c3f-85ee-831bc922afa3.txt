William J. Clinton
January 11, 1999

Remarks Following the Entertainment at the State Dinner Honoring President Carlos Menem of Argentina
President Clinton.Well, we want to say, first of all, thank you to our wonderful musicians, our magnificent dancers. I will never look at you again in quite the same way, Robert. [Laughter]
We are very grateful to you, and I hope, Mr. President, you feel a little more at home. Thank you, again.
NOTE: The President spoke at 10:50 p.m. in the East Room at the White House. In his remarks, he referred to actor Robert Duvall. The transcript released by the Office of the Press Secretary also included the remarks of President Menem.
