William J. Clinton
June 04, 1993

Letter to Congressional Leaders Reporting Budget Rescissions
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment control Act of 1974, I herewith report six proposed rescissions, totaling $176.0 million in budgetary resources.
These proposed rescissions affect the Departments of Housing and Urban Development, Justice, and Transportation. The details of the proposed rescissions are contained in the attached reports.
Sincerely,
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate. The reports detailing the proposed rescissions were published in the Federal Register on June 15.
