William J. Clinton
June 15, 1999

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Commodity Credit Corporation
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the provisions of section 13, Public Law 806, 80th Congress (15 U.S.C. 714k), I transmit herewith the report of the Commodity Credit Corporation for the fiscal year ending September 30, 1997.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
