William J. Clinton
June 24, 1996

Message to the Congress Reporting a Budget Deferral
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one revised deferral of budgetary resources, totaling $7.4 million. The deferral affects the Social Security Administration.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, June 24, 1996.
NOTE: The report detailing the deferral was published in theFederal Registeron July 3.
