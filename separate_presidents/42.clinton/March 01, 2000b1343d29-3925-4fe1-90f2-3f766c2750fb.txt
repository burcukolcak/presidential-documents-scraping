William J. Clinton
March 01, 2000

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Trade Policy Agenda and the Trade Agreements Program Report
To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 163 of the Trade Act of 1974, as amended (19 U.S.C. 2213), I transmit herewith the 2000 Trade Policy Agenda and 1999 Annual Report on the Trade Agreements Program. The Report, as required by sections 122, 124, and 125 of the Uruguay Round Agreements Act, includes the Annual Report on the World Trade Organization and a 5-year assessment of the U.S. participation in the World Trade Organization.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, March 1, 2000.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on March 2.
