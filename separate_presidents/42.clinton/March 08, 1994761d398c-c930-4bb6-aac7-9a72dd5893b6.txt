William J. Clinton
March 08, 1994

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Corporation for Public Broadcasting
To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 19(3) of Public Telecommunications Act of 1992 (Public Law 102-356), I transmit herewith the report of the Corporation for Public Broadcasting.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, March 8, 1994.
