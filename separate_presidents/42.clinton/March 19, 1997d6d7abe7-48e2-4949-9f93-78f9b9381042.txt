William J. Clinton
March 19, 1997

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Budget Rescission
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one proposed rescission of budgetary resources, totaling $10 million.
The proposed rescission affects the Department of Energy.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House,
March 19, 1997.
NOTE: The report detailing the proposed rescission was published in theFederal Registeron March 26.
