William J. Clinton
May 02, 1995

Message to the Congress Reporting Budget Rescissions
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report three rescission proposals, totaling $132.0 million.
The proposed rescissions affect the Departments of Justice and Transportation, and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House,
May 2, 1995.
NOTE: The report detailing the proposed rescissions was published in theFederal Registeron May 9.
