William J. Clinton
May 02, 1997

Exchange With Reporters on the Budget Agreement
Q.Mr. President, have you got all the Democrats on board now?
The President.A lot of them. I feel good about it.
Q.Do you need to do a selling job to the American people? Do you think you need to sell this at all?
The President.Oh, sure. We need to talk about it. We will. It's a good agreement, a good thing for America.
Q.[Inaudible]
The President.We're having them over here for a while.
NOTE: The exchange began at 6:05 p.m. on the South Lawn at the White House, on the President's return from Baltimore, MD. A tape was not available for verification of the content of this exchange.
