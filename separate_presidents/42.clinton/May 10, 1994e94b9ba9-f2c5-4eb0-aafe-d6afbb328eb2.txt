William J. Clinton
May 10, 1994

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Department of Housing and Urban Development
To the Congress of the United States:
Pursuant to the requirements of 42 U.S.C. 3536, I transmit herewith the 28th Annual Report of the Department of Housing and Urban Development, which covers calendar year 1992.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, May 10, 1994.
