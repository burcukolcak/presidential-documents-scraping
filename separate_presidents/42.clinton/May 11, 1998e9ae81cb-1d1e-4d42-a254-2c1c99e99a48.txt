William J. Clinton
May 11, 1998

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the National Institute of Building Sciences
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the requirements of section 809 of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974, as amended (12 U.S.C. 1701j-2(j)), I transmit herewith the annual report of the National Institute of Building Sciences for fiscal year 1996.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, May 11, 1998.
