William J. Clinton
May 14, 1996

Message to the Congress Reporting a Budget Deferral
To the Congress of the United States:
In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, I herewith report one revised deferral of budgetary resources, totaling $1.4 billion. The deferral affects the International Security Assistance program.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, May 14, 1996.
NOTE: The report detailing the deferral was published in theFederal Registeron May 24.
