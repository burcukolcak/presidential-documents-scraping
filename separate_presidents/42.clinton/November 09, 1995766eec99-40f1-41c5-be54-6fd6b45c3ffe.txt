William J. Clinton
November 09, 1995

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the National Corporation for Housing Partnerships
To the Congress of the United States:
I transmit herewith the annual report of the National Corporation for Housing Partnerships and the National Housing Partnership for fiscal years 1993 and 1994, as required by section 3938(a)(1) of title 42 of the United States Code.
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House, November 9, 1995.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on November 10.
