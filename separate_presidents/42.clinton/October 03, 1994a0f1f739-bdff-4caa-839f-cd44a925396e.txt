William J. Clinton
October 03, 1994

Message to the Congress Transmitting Transportation Department Reports
To the Congress of the United States:
I transmit herewith the 1993 calendar year reports as prepared by the Department of Transportation on activities under the Highway Safety Act and the National Traffic and Motor Vehicle Safety Act of 1966, as amended (23 U.S.C. 401 note and 15 U.S.C. 1408).
WILLIAM J. CLINTON
The White House,
October 3, 1994.
