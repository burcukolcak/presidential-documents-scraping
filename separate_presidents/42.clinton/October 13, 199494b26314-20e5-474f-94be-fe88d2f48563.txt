William J. Clinton
October 13, 1994

Statement on the National Award for Museum Service
This national honor is a tribute to the power of museums to engage children, families, and communities, in towns and cities all across America.
NOTE: This statement was included in a White House statement announcing the presentation of the National Award for Museum Service to the Brukner Nature Center, Troy, OH; the Cummer Museum of Art and Gardens, Jacksonville, FL; and the Missouri Historical Society, St. Louis, MO.
