William J. Clinton
October 28, 1998

Statement on Emergency Funding for Antidrug Activities
I am making an additional $732 million in emergency funding available to support antidrug activities and drug interdiction. These funds were provided in the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1999, which I signed into law on Wednesday, October 21st.
