George W. Bush
August 09, 2007

Memorandum on Reimbursement for Former President Gerald R. Ford Funeral Expenses
Memorandum for the Director of the Office of Management and Budget
Subject:Reimbursement for Former President Gerald R. Ford Funeral Expenses
Pursuant to the authority vested in me as the President by the Constitution and the laws of the United States including Public Law 110-5, the Revised Continuing Appropriations Resolution, 2007, I hereby authorize the expenditure from the appropriation, "Unanticipated Needs:"

To defray expenses incurred as a result of the funeral services for former President Gerald R. Ford.
I hereby determine that this expenditure is necessary to meet unanticipated needs in furtherance of the national interest.
GEORGE W. BUSH
