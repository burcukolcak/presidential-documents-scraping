George W. Bush
December 17, 2004

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the "U.S. Ocean Action Plan"
Dear Mr. Speaker: (Dear Mr. President:)
Consistent with section 4 of the Oceans Act of 2000 (Public Law 106-256; 33 U.S.C. 857-19), I transmit herewith the "U.S. Ocean Action Plan," a report and statement of proposals prepared by the Council on Environmental Quality in response to the Commission on Ocean Policy's final recommendations.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Richard B. Cheney, President of the Senate.
