George W. Bush
February 11, 2004

Letter to the Speaker of the House of Representatives Transmitting Appropriations Requests for the Judicial Branch
Dear Mr. Speaker:
As a matter of comity, I am transmitting to the Congress the enclosed requests from the Judicial Branch for FY 2004.
The details of these requests are set forth in the enclosed letter from the Director of the Office of Management and Budget.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
NOTE: This letter was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on February 12.
