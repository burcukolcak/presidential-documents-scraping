George W. Bush
July 09, 2002

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting an Account of Federal Expenditures for Climate Change Programs and Activities
Dear Mr. Chairman:
In accordance with section 559(b) of the Foreign Operations, Export Financing, and Related Programs Appropriations Act, 2002 (Public Law 107-115), I transmit herewith an account of Federal expenditures for climate change programs and activities. This report includes both domestic and international programs and activities related to climate change, and associated costs by line item as presented in the President's Budget Appendix.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Robert C. Byrd, chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations, and C.W. Bill Young, chairman, House Committee on Appropriations.
