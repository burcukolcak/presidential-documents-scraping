George W. Bush
June 06, 2002

Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the Interagency Arctic Research Policy Committee
To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 108(b) of Public Law 98-373 (15 U.S.C. 4107(b)), I transmit herewith the Ninth Biennial Report of the Interagency Arctic Research Policy Committee (February 1, 2000, to January 31, 2002).
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, June 6, 2002.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on June 11.
