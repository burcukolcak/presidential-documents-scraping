George W. Bush
March 06, 2001

Message to the Congress Transmitting the Trade Policy Agenda and a Report on the Trade Agreements Program
To the Congress of the United States:
As required by section 163 of the Trade Act of 1974, as amended (19 U.S.C. 2213), I transmit herewith the 2001 Trade Policy Agenda and 2000 Annual Report on the Trade Agreements Program.
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, March 6, 2001.
