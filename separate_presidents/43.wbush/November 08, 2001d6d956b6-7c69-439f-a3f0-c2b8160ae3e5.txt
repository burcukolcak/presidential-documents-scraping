George W. Bush
November 08, 2001

Message to the Congress Transmitting Reports of the Department of Transportation
To the Congress of the United States:
I transmit herewith the Department of Transportation's Calendar Year 1999 reports on Activities Under the National Traffic and Motor Vehicle Safety Act of 1966, the Highway Safety Act of 1966, and the Motor Vehicle Information and Cost Savings Act of 1972.
GEORGE W. BUSH
The White House, November 8, 2001.
NOTE: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on November 9.
