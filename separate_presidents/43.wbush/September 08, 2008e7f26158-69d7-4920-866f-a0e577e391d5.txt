George W. Bush
September 08, 2008

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Proliferation Detection and Interdiction Activities
Dear__________:
Consistent with section 10(b)(1) of the Department of State Authorities Act of 2006 (Public Law 109-472), I am pleased to transmit the enclosed report on proliferation detection and interdiction activities. This report, prepared by the Department of State, includes information on efforts to provide proliferation detection and interdiction assistance to friendly foreign countries.
Sincerely,
GEORGE W. BUSH
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Joseph R. Biden, Jr., chairman, and Richard G. Lugar, ranking member, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; and Howard L. Berman, chairman, and Ileana Ros-Lehtinen, ranking member, House Committee on Foreign Affairs.
