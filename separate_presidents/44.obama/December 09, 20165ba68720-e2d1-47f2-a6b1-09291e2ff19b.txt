Memorandum for the Secretary of State
Subject:Minnesota World's Fair 2023: Wellness and Well Being for All
In accordance with Public Law 91-269, I have approved the recommendation for Federal recognition of the "Minnesota World's Fair 2023: Wellness and Well Being for All," finding that such recognition will be in the national interest.
Accordingly, I am requesting you to notify the Bureau of International Expositions of my actions.
BARACK OBAMA
