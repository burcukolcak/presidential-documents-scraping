Donald J. Trump
February 22, 2019

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on United States Strategy for Syria
Dear Mr. Chairman:
In accordance with section 1221 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (Public Law 115-91) and section 1231 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 (Public Law 115-232), I have enclosed a report describing the United States Strategy for Syria.
Sincerely,
DONALD J. TRUMP
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to James M. Inhofe, chairman, Senate Committee on Armed Services; James E. Risch, chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; D. Adam Smith, chairman, House Committee on Armed Services; and Eliot L. Engel, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs. The letter was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on February 25.
