Donald J. Trump
May 14, 2018

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on United States Strategy Toward Yemen
Dear Mr. Chairman:
Pursuant to section 1275 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (Public Law 115-91), I am forwarding a report describing the U.S. Strategy for Yemen.
Sincerely,

DONALD J. TRUMP

Corker, Jr., chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; Richard M. Burr, chairman, Senate Select Committee on Intelligence; Rodney P. Frelinghuysen, chairman, House Committee on Appropriations; William "Mac" Thornberry, chairman, House Committee on Armed Services; Edward R. Royce, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs; and Devin G. Nunes, chairman, House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence.
