Joseph R. Biden
April 26, 2021

Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Statement Showing Apportionment Population for Each State
Dear Madam Speaker: (Dear Madam President:)
Pursuant to title 2, United States Code, section 2a(a), I transmit herewith the statement showing the apportionment population for each State as of April 1, 2020, and the number of Representatives to which each State would be entitled.
Sincerely,
JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Nancy Pelosi, Speaker of the House of Representatives, and Kamala D. Harris, President of the Senate. An original was not available for verification of the content of this letter.
