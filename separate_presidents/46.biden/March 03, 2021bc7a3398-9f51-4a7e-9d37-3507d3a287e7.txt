Joseph R. Biden
March 03, 2021

Letter to Congressional Leaders on the Annual Report on Legal and Policy Frameworks Guiding the United States Use of Military Force and Related National Security Operations
Dear Mr. Chairman: (Dear Madam Chair:)
In accordance with section 1264 of the National Defense Authorization Act for fiscal year 2018 (Public Law 115-91), as amended, 50 U.S.C. 1549(a), I am transmitting the annual report on the legal and policy frameworks guiding the United States' use of military force and related national security operations.
I am enclosing a copy of the unclassified notice, as well as its classified annex.
JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.
NOTE: Identical letters were sent to Patrick J. Leahy, chairman, Senate Committee on Appropriations; John F. Reed, chairman, Senate Armed Services Committee; Robert Menendez, chairman, Senate Committee on Foreign Relations; Mark R. Warner, chairman, Senate Select Committee on Intelligence; Rosa L. DeLauro, chairwoman, House Committee on Appropriations; D. Adam Smith, chairman, House Committee on Armed Services; Gregory W. Meeks, chairman, House Committee on Foreign Affairs; and Adam B. Schiff, chairman, House Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence. An original was not available for verification of the content of this letter.
