Joseph R. Biden
November 12, 2021

Remarks in an Exchange With Reporters Prior to Departure for Camp David, Maryland
Influx of Migrants at the Belarus-Poland Border
Q.What do you think about the situation on the border between Poland and Belarus?
The President.I think it's of great concern. We've communicated our concern to Russia; we've communicated our concern to Belarus. And we think it's a problem.
NOTE: The President spoke at 5:33 p.m. on the South Lawn at the White House prior to boarding Marine One.
