James Monroe
April 10, 1818

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliancewith a resolution of the Senate respecting the supplies of the Northwestern army, within certain periods therein specified, by contractors, commissaries, and agents, and the expense thereby incurred, I now transmit to them a report from the Secretary of War, which, with the documents accompanying it, will afford the information required.
JAMES MONROE.
