James Monroe
May 06, 1822

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 26th of April, requesting the President of the United States "tocommunicate to the Senate the report of the Attorney-General relative to any persons (citizens of the United States) who have been charged with or suspected of introducing any slaves into the United States contrary to existing laws," I transmit herewith two reports from the Attorney-General.
JAMES MONROE.
