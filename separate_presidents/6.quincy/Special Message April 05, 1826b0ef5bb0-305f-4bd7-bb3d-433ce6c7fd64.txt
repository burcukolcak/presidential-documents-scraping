John Quincy Adams
April 05, 1826

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the House of the 30th ultimo, I transmit to the House a report* from the Secretary of State, with the documents desired by the resolution; and also a copy of the letter from the Secretary of State to Mr. Poinsett acknowledging the receipt of his dispatch No. 22, accidentally overlooked in the answer to the resolution of the House of the 27th ultimo.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*Relating to the proposed congress of the Spanish American States.
