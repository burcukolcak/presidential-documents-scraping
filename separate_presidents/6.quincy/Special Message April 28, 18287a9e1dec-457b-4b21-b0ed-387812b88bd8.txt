John Quincy Adams
April 28, 1828

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 9th instant, requesting a communication of the correspondence between this Government and that of Great Britain on the subject of the trade between the United States and the British colonial possessions in the West Indies and North America, not heretofore communicated, I transmit to the House a report from the Secretary of State, with the correspondence desired.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
