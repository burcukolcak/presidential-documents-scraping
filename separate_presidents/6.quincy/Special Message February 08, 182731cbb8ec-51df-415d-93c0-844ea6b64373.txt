John Quincy Adams
February 08, 1827

Special Message
To the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States:
I communicate to Congress copies of a letter from the governor of the State of Georgia, received since my message of the 5th instant, and of inclosures received with it, further confirmative of the facts stated in that message.*
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*Relating to the conflicting claims of Georgia and the Creek Indians to lands in Georgia.
