John Quincy Adams
February 26, 1829

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 20th instant, I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of War, with the inspection reports of Brevet Major-General Gaines for the years 1826 and 1827, relating to the organization of the Army and militia of the United States, with the request that the original documents may be returned to the Department of War at the convenience of the Senate.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
