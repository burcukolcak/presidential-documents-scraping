John Quincy Adams
January 09, 1828

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States.
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 7th instant, I transmit herewith Mitchell's map and the map marked A,* as requested by the resolution, desiring that when the Senate shall have no further use for them they may be returned.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*Relating to the northeastern boundary of the United States.
