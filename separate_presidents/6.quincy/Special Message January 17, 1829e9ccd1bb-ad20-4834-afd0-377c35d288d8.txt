John Quincy Adams
January 17, 1829

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the House of Representatives of the 13th instant, I transmit herewith a report* from the Secretary of War, with an application from the Creek Indians, through the agent of the United States, and an opinion of counsel in behalf of the Indians, having relation to the subject of the resolution.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*Relating to claims of Georgia and the Creek Indians under the treaty of 1821, held at Indian Springs.
