John Quincy Adams
March 25, 1828

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of State, prepared in compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 25th of February last, requesting copies of instructions and correspondence relating to the settlement of the boundary lines of the United States, or any one of them, under the Government of the Confederated States and by the definitive treaty of peace of 3d September, 1783, with Great Britain.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
