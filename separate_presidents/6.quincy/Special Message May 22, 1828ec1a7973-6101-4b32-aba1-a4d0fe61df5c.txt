John Quincy Adams
May 22, 1828

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
The inclosed report from the Secretary of State is accompanied by copies of the correspondence between this Government and the minister of His Britannic Majesty residing here relating to the arrest and imprisonment of John Baker,* requested by a recent resolution of the House.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
*By the authorities of the Province of New Brunswick.
