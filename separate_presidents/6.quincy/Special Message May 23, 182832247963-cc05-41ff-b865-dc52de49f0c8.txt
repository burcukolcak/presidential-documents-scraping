John Quincy Adams
May 23, 1828

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 8th instant, relating to the accounts and official conduct of Thomas A. Smith, receiver of public moneys at Franklin, Mo., I transmit herewith a report from the Secretary of the Treasury, with documents, containing the information desired by the resolution.
JOHN QUINCY ADAMS.
