Andrew Jackson
February 28, 1835

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I transmit to the Senate of the United States a report* of the Secretary of State, to whom was referred the resolutions of that body passed on the 2d and 17th days of the present month, together with such portion of the correspondence and instructions requested by the said resolutions as could be transcribed within the time that has elapsed since they were received and as can be communicated without prejudice to the public interest
*Relating to the treaty of indemnity with Spain of February 17, 1834.
ANDREW JACKSON
