Martin van Buren
April 10, 1840

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
In compliance with the resolution of the House of Representatives of the 23d March last, I transmit a, report* from the Secretary of State, which, with the documents accompanying it, contains the information in possession of the Department in relation to the subject of the resolution.
M. VAN BUREN.
*Relating to the seizure and condemnation by British authorities of American vessels engaged in the fisheries.
