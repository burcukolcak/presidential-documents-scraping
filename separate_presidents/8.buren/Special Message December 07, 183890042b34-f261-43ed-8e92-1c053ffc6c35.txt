Martin van Buren
December 07, 1838

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the House of Representatives reports * from the Secretary of State and the Secretary of the Treasury, with accompanying documents, in answer to the resolution of the House of the 9th of July last.
M. VAN BUREN.
*Transmitting communications, papers. documents. etc., elucidating the origin and objects of the Smithsonian bequest and the origin, progress, and consummation of the process by which that bequest was recovered, etc,
