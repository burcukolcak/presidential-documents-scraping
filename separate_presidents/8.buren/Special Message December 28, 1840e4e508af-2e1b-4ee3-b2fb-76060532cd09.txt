Martin van Buren
December 28, 1840

Special Message
To the House of Representatives of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the House of Representatives a report** from the Secretary of State, with accompanying papers, in answer to their resolution of the 21st instant.
M. VAN BUREN.
**Transmitting correspondence with Great Britain relative to the burning of the steamboatCaroline at Schlosser, N. Y, December 29, 1837.
