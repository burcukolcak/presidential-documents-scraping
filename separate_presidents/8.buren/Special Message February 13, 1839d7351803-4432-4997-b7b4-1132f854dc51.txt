Martin van Buren
February 13, 1839

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
I herewith transmit to the Senate a report++ from the Secretary of State, with accompanying documents, in answer to their resolution of the 1st instant.
M. VAN BUREN.
(++Relating to compensation by Great Britain in the cases of the brigsEnterprise, Encomium,andComet, slaves on board which were forcibly seized and detained by local authorities of Bermuda and Bahama islands)
