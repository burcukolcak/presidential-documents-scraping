Martin van Buren
January 15, 1839

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
In compliance with a resolution of the Senate of the 9th of July last, I transmit reports* from the several Departments of the Government to which that resolution was referred.
M. VAN BUREN.
*Transmitting statements of cases in which a per centum has been allowed to public officers on disbursements of public moneys.
