Martin van Buren
January 25, 1840

Special Message
To the Senate of the United States:
The accompanying report ++ from the Secretary of State is, with its inclosures, communicated to the Senate in compliance with their resolution of the 14th instant.
M. VAN BUREN.
++Relating to the compensation by Great Britain in the case of the brigsEnterprise, Encomium , andComet, slaves on board which were forcibly seized and detained by local authorities of Bermuda and Bahama islands.
