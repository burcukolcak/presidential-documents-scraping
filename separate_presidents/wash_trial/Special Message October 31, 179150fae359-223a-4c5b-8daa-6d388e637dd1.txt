George Washington
October 31, 1791

Special Message
Gentlemen of the Senate and of the House of Representatives:
I send you herewith the arrangement which has been made by pursuant to the act entitled "An act repealing after the last day of June next the duties heretofore laid upon distilled spirits imported from abroad and laying others in their stead, and also upon spirits distilled within the United States, and for appropriating the same," in respect to the subdivision of the several districts created by the said act into surveys of inspection, the appointment of officers for the same, and the assignment of compensations.
GO. WASHINGTON.
